Boston 40th Anniversary Tour Dates

September 17, 2024
blog

LOS ANGELES (CelebrityAccess) — Boston announced a string of dates from April through August to celebrate the 40th anniversary of their classic debut album.


The tour begins at Hard Rock Live in Hollywood Florida on April 29th and will end on August 11th in Uncasville, CT at The Mohegan Sun Arena.
To make the tour slightly different the merchandise will include a new “’70s-inspired graphic” which will be chosen through a contest going on now. The winner not only gets to see their art at the merch booth but they will also receive $1,000 prize (plus a pair of tickets and backstage passes to the tour date of their choice. Second and third place will also receive tickets and passes to the show of their choosing
The deadline for submissions in the contest is March 23, 2016. For official rules and entry go to bandboston.com

4/30 – Clearwater, Fla. – Ruth Eckerd Hall

5/07 – Baltimore, Md. – Pier Six Pavilion
5/18 – Cleveland, Ohio – Jacobs Pavilion At Nautica
5/21 – New Buffalo, Mich. – Four Winds Casino / Silver Creek Event Center
5/25 – Huber Heights, Ohio – Rose Music Center At The Heights

8/04 – Baton Rouge, La. – River Center Arena
8/09 – Bethlehem, Pa. – Bethlehem Musikfest
8/11 – Uncasville, Ct. – Mohegan Sun Arena
