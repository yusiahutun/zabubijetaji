    Han Chong has launched a debut bridal collection for Self-Portrait spring/summer 2016. The gowns, made from guipure lace, silk and organza in shades of white and cream, follow the same feminine but fashion-forward aesthetic. Styles range from high-necked Victorian-inspired collars with ruffled skirts to off-the-shoulder dresses with asymmetric hems. The collection is priced from £650 to £1,000 and will be available to buy in Selfridges, Bergdorf Goodman, Galeries Lafayette and on Net-a-Porter.com and Self-portrait-studio.com.
