It's a dead cert that her over-protective mafioso onscreen father would not approve.

But former Sopranos star Jamie Lynn Sigler, 29, has clearly left Meadow Soprano behind as she stepped out in a plunging strapless dress to promote her appearance in a new Off-Broadway show.

Showing off her not inconsiderable charms, she looked a slip away from a potentially embarrassing wardrobe malfunction.

New look: Jamie Lynn Sigler has shed her Sopranos image with a grown up look in a daring plunging minidress as she celebrated her new play in New York

Jamie-Lynn wore the nude strapless dress as she joined the Off-Broadway cast of Nora and Delia Ephron's Love, Loss, and What I Wore on Sept. 1 at the Westside Theatre.

Plunging: Jamie Lynn was a whisker away from a wardrobe malfunction

And with her lightened hair, she looked more glamorous than ever as she prepares to take to the stage opposite Tony Award-winning actress Victoria Clark, Helen Carey, Nancy Giles, Stacy London.

 The play is based on the best-selling book by Ilene Beckerman that 'uses clothing and accessories and the memories they trigger to tell funny and often poignant stories that all women can relate to.'

The young actress has been off the radar for a little while since enjoying the high-profile success of the Emmy award-winning show. But that could all be about to change.

She has been approached to star in a pilot for a new police show.

Jamie Lynn will star in the story of San Diego police detective Molly Collins (Stringfield) who investigates crimes with her partner Brooke Kross (Sigler) - all while trying to juggle a hectic personal life.

 The twenty-nine-year-old actress has appeared in a number of other theatre productions, including The Wizard of Oz and The Diary of Anne Frank.

Looking good: The actress went all nude with her minidress and patent heels as she prepares to take to the stage in Love, Loss And What I Wore

While she hasn't had a regular-series part since appearing in The Sopranos. However, more recently she has been seen on the HBO series Entourage.

Jamie Lynn and a rotating cast of actresses will appear in Love, Loss, and What I Wore until October 3.
