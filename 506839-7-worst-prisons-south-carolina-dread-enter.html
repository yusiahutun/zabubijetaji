1. Lieber Correctional Institution

Leiber Correctional Institution is the most violent prison in the world. This is a maximum security prison that houses male prisoners. The facility is located in Ridgeville, Dorchester County. It was established in 1986 and houses more than 1400 inmates.

In recent years, the facility has made headlines for the wrong reasons. Incidences of violence have erupted in the facility, culminating in several inmates' deaths. In 2009, two death row inmates attacked a correctional officer and stabbed him using homemade knives.

2. Lee Correctional Facility

Lee Correctional Facility is one of the most dangerous prisons in South Carolina. The maximum security facility was established in 1993. It is located in Bishopville, South Carolina, and houses more than 1700 male inmates.

The facility has also made headlines for the wrong reasons. Cases of inmates being killed by their fellow inmates in the facility are rampant. In 2018, a deadly riot took place at the facility leaving seven prisoners dead and 22 injured. This incident is one of the deadliest prison riots. In 2016, one inmate died, and another was wounded during a stabbing incident with homemade knives.

3. Kirkland Correctional Institution

The Kirkland prison in South Carolina was established in 1975. It is located in Columbia, Richland County, and houses male inmates. There are more than 1700 inmates in the facility. The facility has reported incidences of violence that have culminated in the deaths of several inmates. In 2017, four prisoners were killed at the facility after two fellow inmates lured them into a cell.

4. Evans correctional institution

Evans Correctional Institution is a level 2 medium security facility located in Bennettsville, South Carolina. The facility was established in 1989 and houses more than 1300 inmates. Cases of inmates being violently abused and physically tortured have been reported at the facility.

In 2017, one inmate lost his life when he was stabbed in an inmate-on-inmate violence incident. Several other inmates were injured during the incident.

5. Perry Correctional Institution

Perry Correctional Institution is one of the maximum security prisons in South Carolina. It is located in Pelzer, South Carolina. The facility was established in 1981, and it houses approximately 1000 prisoners. The facility has reported several incidences of riots and violence.
