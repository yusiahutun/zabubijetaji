Best Ferris Bueller Costume Ideas

June 08, 2024
blog

Life moves pretty fast. If don't stop and look around once in a while, you could miss...Halloween! If October 31 has snuck up on you (again!) and you need a last-minute Halloween costume, movie costume ideas are a great place to startâ€”namely one of the most iconic films of the '80s, Ferris Bueller's Day Off. From righteous dude Ferris Bueller himself or his brooding best friend Cameron Frye to Ferris's sophisticated sweetheart Sloane Peterson or his jealous sister Jeanie Bueller, there's no shortage of memorable male and female characters to re-create.

What's especially fun about this costume theme is that it works to go solo or team up for a best friend Halloween costume. Dress up as pals Ferris Bueller in his signature jacket and Cameron Frye in his Detroit Red Wings hockey jersey. Choose a Ferris Bueller outfit and stylish Sloane Peterson getup for a Halloween costume for couples. Or better yet, get the whole gang together and go as Ferris, Cameron, Sloane, and Jeanie!

Have even more fun with some of the movie's most memorable scenes and carry around a toy car Ferrari as a nod to Cameron's dad's beloved sportscar. Having a party? Serve sausages to pay homage to the Sausage King of Chicago, Abe Froman. Create a soundtrack that includes "Danke Schoen" and "Twist and Shout" to emulate the parade float scene, followed by "Oh Yeah" (Bow bow chick chicka chickaaa).

So this Halloween, funnel all of that '80s energy from this classic movie into a DIY Ferris Bueller Halloween costume. Bonus: You likely already have some of these items in your closet or drawers. Oh yeah!
