Did Cody Ko date OnlyFans star Corinna Kopf? YouTuber gets engaged to Kelsey Kreppel

May 26, 2024

YouTuber Cody Ko recently announced his engagement to girlfriend and influencer Kelsey Kreppel via a romantic Instagram post. A series of photos showed the 31-year-old Canadian YouTuber getting down on one knee in a beautiful beach setting, as his girlfriend gasps in surprise and disbelief. "Yesterday was a beautiful perfect day. thanks for making me the happiest guy on earth @kelseykreppel," Cody captioned the post.

Meanwhile, Kelsey also shared candid snaps of the couple where she can be seen flaunting the big rock as Cody plants a kiss on her cheek. She wrote, "I’ve never been more sure about anything. can’t wait to spend forever smiling with you." The happy couple was congratulated by famous social media celebrities as well as their overjoyed fans. However, a handful of internet users did not miss the chance to remind everyone that Cody Ko was once rumored to be with fellow YouTuber and OnyFans model Corinna Kopf before he met Kelsey.

At one point, Corinna Kopf and Cody Ko regularly appeared in each other's videos. They appeared to be close friends and often interacted with each other in Twitter threads and post comments. Their candid and fun camaraderie prompted many fans to speculate that they were dating each other. Though the duo never confirmed or denied the rumors, their romance was short-lived if at all there was something. 

"I read somewhere that he dated Corinna Kopf for a little bit but nothing serious. But I feel bad talking about something I just read but have no idea if it’s true," a Reddit user remarked in a thread discussing Cody Ko's exes. 

Another Redditor shared a 2017 video by David Dobrik, where he referred to Corinna as Cody's girlfriend. "I’m a big David Dobrik fan and am laying here watching old videos and I came across this one which reminded me of your comment and David does call Corinna Cody’s GF," a user noted. 

It can be assumed that Cody and Corinna split shortly before the former met his present girlfriend Kelsey. Cody first mentioned her in a podcast from July 2017, where he said he was cooking on Taco Tuesday for a girl he liked. His crush turned out to be none other than Kelsey and they soon hit it off. The pair have been inseparable ever since, and are well-known for their romantic gestures and cute antics with each other. 

Following the announcement of their engagement, Twitter was flooded with congratulatory comments, as YouTuber duo Chicks in The Office noted, "Congratulations to Kelsey Kreppel & Cody Ko on their engagement." Another fan wrote, "cody and kelsey are literally perfect for each other like they’re the epitome of soulmates."

"CODY KO AND KELSEY R FINALLY ENGAGED !!! this is the best news i’ve heard all year," a third fan tweeted. Another overjoyed fan shared, "im just so happy cody and kelsey got engagedddd i can't stop smilingggg."
