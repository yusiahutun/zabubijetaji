How Much Is Sierra Gates' Net Worth?

Well, Sierra Gates has a lot on her plate right now. On the one hand, she is a well-established entrepreneur, and on the other, she is a reality star and a social media sensation.

Sierra Gates' Husband, Kids

Unlike her fantastic career, Sierra's love life has not exactly been a smooth ride.

As of now, the reality star is in a relationship with Love & Hip Hop co-star BK Brasco. The couple has been together in an on and off-again relationship for the past two years.

Before BK, Sierra was married to Rod Gates (known as Shooter) for eight years. But the marriage broke apart after Sierra found out about Shooter cheating on her with her assistant. The news came out during their time in the reality show Love and Hip Hop, which steered quite a drama on TV.

The estranged couple also had a son together- named Mason. After the divorce, the couple shared joint custody of their child, with Sierra being the primary legal guardian.
