A North Carolina teacher accused of having sex with a student 46 years her junior has been killed in an apparent murder-suicide.

Emma Ogle, 63, was found shot dead alongside her 59-year-old husband Michael on Wednesday, one week after she was charged with a sex act with a 17-year-old pupil.

A relative went to check on Michael when he didn't turn up for work; police then received a 911 call reporting Ogle was armed and had barricaded herself inside their Huntersville home.
