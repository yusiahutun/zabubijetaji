Walmart's $20 Christmas Tree Collar Is a Dupe for One of Our Favorites

January 28, 2024
blog

The season is upon us. Fall may just now be cooling our temperatures down, but Christmas is already on our minds. Which works out for the best because, as all good holiday shoppers know, the early bird gets the best Christmas deals.

Holiday Time Natural Seagrass Woven Christmas Tree Collar

Holiday Time Natural Seagrass Woven Christmas Tree Collar

Trust us: this one is no exception. Walmart currently has a seagrass tree collar on sale for just $19.98. You read that right: under $20 for a woven tree collar! These beauties are staples in country homes and for that price, you could just get one for every tree. Might we add, this tree collar also looks a good bit like one from one of our favorite, cough cough more expensive, retailers but runs for a fraction of the price.

The collar measures 27" across, so it's perfectly suited to hide the base of a small or medium-sized tree. We think trees 8 feet and under should fit well with this collar, but it's always best to measure the base of your tree just to be sure. Another tip: tree collars work best for artificial trees thanks to their smaller base structure.

Whether it's time to throw out the tattered tree skirt or you've been pining over that more expensive woven collar for years, this inexpensive option fits right in the holiday budget.
