Early life

Casey Anthony was born Casey Marie Anthony on 19 March 1986 (age 38 as of 2024) in Warren, Ohio, United States. She is the daughter of George Anthony (father) and Cindy Anthony (mother). Casey has one sibling, Lee Anthony.

Her father, George Anthony, worked in law enforcement. During her trial, Casey's defence attorney claimed that her father had sexually abused her, beginning when she was eight years old. According to Huff Post, the attorney proceeded further by suggesting that Casey's brother Lee had made sexual advances towards her as well.

Casey Anthony's net worth

Casey Anthony filed for bankruptcy after her trial. In 2013, she agreed to pay $25,000 to settle a dispute over the rights to her life story, borrowing the money from a friend. The settlement was part of an effort to pay off $800,000 of her debts to lawyers and other creditors.

In addition to this settlement, Anthony has also been involved in a dispute with Texas EquuSearch, a non-profit group that assisted in the search for Caylee. The group sued Anthony for fraud and unjust enrichment, estimating it spent more than $100,000 searching for Caylee even though she was already dead.

This response forced Anthony to file for bankruptcy in January 2013, claiming over $1,000 in assets and nearly $800,000 in debt. According to In Touch Weekly and Parade, Casey Anthony's net worth is about $10,000.
