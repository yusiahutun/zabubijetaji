Your Spring Tarot Card Reading, By Zodiac Sign

June 23, 2024
blog

March 20th brings the spring equinox, the beginning of the Aries season, and the astrological new year. With the planetary ingress and the flowers beginning to bud, comes a fresh vibe and energy that we're all welcoming with open arms.

Curious how your zodiac sign will be affected? Check out your tarot 'scope below.

Aries: 3 of Pentacles

With hard work and determination, you can achieve anything. The caveat is that you'll have to collaborate and work with others to make it happen. After all, there is no "I" in team.

Taurus: The Emperor

It's been a long time since you've stood up and taken matters into your own hands. You're making the rules and calling the shots in your life now. Through self-discipline and maintaining boundaries with others, you can make anything happen.

Gemini: Ace of Disks/Pentacles

A promising new partnership from business, or in your personal life, is beginning to take flight. Give this budding relationship a lot of TLC and energy to ensure the results align with your goals.

Cancer: Page of Swords

There is a lot of enthusiasm around projects that are happening or in the works. But, don't be surprised if delays occur. Take a step back and relax when things don't happen immediately and take extra time.

Leo: Strength

The drama that's been going on lately is coming to an end. Now, you can move into a new season with a fresh mentality and perspective on situations and relationships. The more control you have over your reactions to things, the better you will feel when confrontation occurs with others.

Virgo: 5 of Wands

All the pent-up anxiety and tension that's been building up is beginning to stress you out. Before you lash out at others, take a breath and calm yourself down. You can attract more bees with honey.

Libra: The Magician

Rigidity definitely does not suit you, Libra. Expand your mind to understand others on a different frequency and allow yourself to experience new situations. Getting out of your comfort zone will do you some good in the next few months.

Scorpio: 2 of Cups

It has been a rocky road towards harmony and peace in your relationships. Now, the tides are turning and order has been restored. Enjoy the romantic vibes and love that is coming your way this spring. Don't take the pleasant vibes for granted right now. Remember where you started.

Sagittarius: The Lovers

A relationship may be at its crossroads, urging you to decide how you would like to move forward. Before you opt to simply play it by ear, keep in mind that you have to make a choice at some point. It isn't necessarily today, but the moment is coming soon.

Capricorn: Page of Wands

A romance or opportunity is coming your way. But, it may not pan out as you wanted because your interest will fade. In order to keep the momentum going strong, it's important to maintain balance and structure by not giving all your energy to something or someone way too soon.

Aquarius: 7 of Cups

This isn't a time of taking action, rather a moment in which you may become lost in your dreams. It's ok to live in your own world, as long as you don't forget to take care of your day-to-day responsibilities and obligations. Set bills to auto-pay and be fancy-free.

Pisces: 4 of Swords

You've been going through a lot of emotional and mental stress, which is why you are currently craving time to rest and relax. Take a break from your obligations and focus on yourself. Give yourself time to chill without feeling guilty. The world can wait for you to get your groove back.
