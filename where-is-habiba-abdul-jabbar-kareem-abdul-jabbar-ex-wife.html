Habiba Abdul-Jabbar’s Parents Couldn’t Attend Her Marriage

What was the Reason behind Habiba’s Divorce from her Ex-Husband?

For this reason, Habiba divorced her then-lover in 1973. Later, the former lovers were separated in a religious ceremony, and in 1978, at Kareem’s request, they officially broke off their engagement.
