20 Best Bible verses for wedding anniversary messages and captions

May 01, 2024
blog

A wedding anniversary allows Christians to reflect on the sacred covenant of God concerning marriages. God authors marriages. Therefore, they can only be successful if God is at the centre. So, what are some of the best Bible verses for wedding anniversary messages to use in your cards or gifts?

Bible verses are not mere words but words that give hope, life and provide growth. These verses not only represent the relationship between couples but also the relationship between Christ and the Church. You can use Bible verses to remind each other of your commitment to each other and God's plan for marriage.

20 best Bible verses for wedding anniversary

Marriage, like any relationship, has its challenges. Bible quotes offer encouragement and inspiration to couples facing difficulties, reminding them of God's faithfulness and the strength of their bond.

Wedding anniversary Bible verses for husband

Husbands have a huge role in a marriage. They are tasked to be the leaders just as Christ Jesus leads the Church to God the Father. You can share some Bible verses for the wedding anniversary with them to encourage them on this journey.

1. Song of Songs 8:6

"Place me like a seal over your heart, like a seal on your arm; for love is as strong as death. Its jealousy is as unyielding as the grave. It burns like blazing fire, like a mighty flame."

This is a wonderful verse for your husband on your wedding anniversary. This verse illustrates the magnitude of God's love. Therefore, it is a prayer that he may have the strength to love you with the same fire as God loves.

2. Song of Songs 3:4

"Scarcely had I passed them when I found the one my heart loves. I held him and would not let him go..." Your anniversary marks a year or years of love and commitment.

Sharing this verse with your loved one shows his importance in your life and marriage.

3. 1 Corinthians 13:4-7

"Love is patient; love is kind. It does not envy; it does not boast; it is not proud. It does not dishonour others, is not self-seeking, is not easily angered, and keeps no record of wrongs. Love does not delight in evil but rejoices with the truth. It always protects, trusts, hopes, and perseveres."

4. 1 Peter 3:7

"Husbands, in the same way, be considerate as you live with your wives, and treat them with respect as the weaker partner and as heirs with you of the gracious gift of life so that nothing will hinder your prayers."

This is an instruction to the husband from the Lord. God acknowledges that a woman is a weaker being compared to a man. Therefore, He instructs the man not to take advantage of her weakness but to be his strength, respecting and loving her throughout.

5. Ephesians 5:25-26

6. Song of Solomon 8:7

"Many waters cannot quench love; rivers cannot sweep it away."

Love is the greatest command from God. When you love your spouse completely, challenges may come, but you will be unmoved.

7. Psalm 127:3-5

"Children are a heritage from the Lord, offspring a reward from him. Like arrows in the hands of a warrior are children born in one's youth. Blessed is the man whose quiver is full of them."

The Psalmist reminds you that having kids in your union is okay and that they come from God. This verse can be used with Christian wedding anniversary wishes with Bible verses to remind you that God wants the best for you, including your children.

8. Romans 12:10

"Be devoted to one another in love. Honour one another above yourselves."

This verse is a great reminder that marriage needs commitment. You can write this quote on your anniversary card and let your spouse understand your thoughts.

9. Colossians 3:14

"And over all these virtues put on love, which binds them all together in perfect unity."

Marriage is a journey, and every milestone should be celebrated. Every Christian understands how important love is in every aspect of life. This verse emphasises the power of love. Not just romantic love but Agape or the perfect love of God.

10. Proverbs 16:3

"Commit thy works unto the Lord, and thy thoughts shall be established."

This verse encourages everyone to submit everything to the Lord, for there is a benefit to doing so.
