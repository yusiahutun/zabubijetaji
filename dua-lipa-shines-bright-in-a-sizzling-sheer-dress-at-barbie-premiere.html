Dua Lipa shines bright in a sizzling sheer dress at Barbie premiere

April 01, 2024
blog

Dua Lipa, who plays Mermaid Barbie, turned heads with a sizzling see-through dress at the Barbie premiere in Los Angeles last weekend before the new movie comes out on July 21.

The British singer understood the assignment and showed up in a dazzling sheer dress in a star-studded event to promote Greta Gerwig’s movie alongside celebrity co-stars Margot Robbie, Ryan Gosling, singer Nicki Minaj, and others.

Dua Lipa dazzles at Barbie premiere with sheer dress

The Dance The Night singer made a bold fashion statement at the Barbie World premiere, which took place at Shrine Auditorium and Expo Hall on Sunday, July 9, 2023.

Dua Lipa left little to the imagination when she walked the pink carpet in a completely see-through dress with neutral underwear. Her silver dress was a beautiful contrast to the pink carpet and she completed her Barbie outfit with a silver necklace and earrings to match the overall scheme of her outfit.

The 27-year-old British star completed her look with long heels, which weren’t visible because of the length of the dress, and natural makeup.

Dua Lipa’s fans react to her look

Dua’s daring and stunning look attracted an array of reactions and opinions on Twitter after pictures and videos from the world premiere went viral across different platforms.

“Dance the night away,” one fan commented. “Finally a good look tonight,” someone else wrote.

Another Twitter user asked: “She’s gorgeous and giving Rihanna. Was this the most appropriate for the premiere of a movie about Barbies though?”

“BODY GOALS OMG. She looks way too good,” another fan added.

Dua will appear as a blue-haired Mermaid Barbie in Greta Gerwig’s movie alongside a star-packed cast with the likes of Michael Cera, America Ferrera, Will Ferrell, Kate McKinnon, and others.

Dua channeled her inner Barbie on holiday

The Levitating and Break My Heart songstress channeled her inner Barbie for an exciting night out when she was on a recent holiday in Greece.

“Come on Barbie let’s go party,” she captioned a stunning carousel of images for her 88 million fans on Instagram. The British singer stunned in a short velvet pink dress with long boots and a silver bag.

In the comments, many of her fans expressed their hopes for a new music studio album in the near future and one person joked: “Come on Barbie, drop the album.”
