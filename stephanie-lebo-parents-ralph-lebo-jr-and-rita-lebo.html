Who Are Stephanie Lebo Parents?

Stephanie Lebo was born to her parents — Ralph Lebo Jr and Rita Lebo — in August 1987. She is a Leo.

A resident of Reading, Pennsylvania, Stephanie works as an R&D Specialist 1 at AkzoNobel. Before embarking on her career as a business analyst, she was a beautician.

Stephanie is a loving mother to her daughter, Giuliana Brice. Giuliana, whose Godmother is Normandy Albert, is a teenager.

In September 2021, Stephanie began dating Jon Gosselin, the star of Jon & Kate Plus 8. They met one another at a backyard barbecue. The loveable couple then met for a date at a restaurant. Things escalated quickly after that, leading Jon to claim that he had been waiting for someone like Stephanie for 19 years. Stephanie even earned approval from Jon’s mother.

John, as you may already know, was married to Kate Gosselin until 2009. His most recent girlfriend before going public with Stephanie was Collen Conrad.

Ralph Lebo Jr is the father of Stephanie Lebo.

Every memory is worth remembering because of you. You did everything with me. You are more than a great dad, you’re an even greater pop-pop. Meet Rita Lebo, Stephanie Lebo Mother

Stephanie Lebo calls Rita Lebo her mother.

Rita is the daughter of Margaret Kohl. Daughter of George W. and Margaret (Doyle) Bausman, Margaret passed away in 2009. Until her retirement, she worked for Meridian Bank.

When Stephanie went to Margaret’s cemetery with flowers, Rita couldn’t contain her happiness as she took to Facebook and wrote, “My daughter took it upon herself to take care of the cemetery this year. I am SO proud of who my daughter has become. I Miss you Mom everyday”

Margaret had 2 more kids — both sons, John P Tabakelis Jr, and George P Tabakelis — with Rita’s father. She also had a stepson with Raymond K Kohl (Rita’s stepfather).

A Korean War veteran, Raymond passed away in 2015 at the age of 83. He worked for Firestone Tire & Rubber Co. in Pottstown and was a bus driver for the Reading School District, (BCIU).
