    What to Look for in a Polo Cologne?

    Season: Ideally, the best Polo colognes for wintertime are warm and spicy. Fresher scents that are clean, aquatic, or citrusy, like Polo Sport, are best suited to hot months. This isn’t a hard and fast rule, just something to keep in mind so you don’t overpower a room or atmosphere.

    Family of Scent: The best Ralph Lauren colognes have mass appeal because they are well-balanced, but scents do vary. You can count on none of them being overly sweet or musky. If anything, Polo colognes lean towards masculine and herbaceous due to its woodsy note. Some scents are loaded with fresh notes, citrusy notes, aquatic notes, and floral notes.

    Long-Lasting: Fortunately, all of the best Polo fragrances with intense middle notes are long-lasting, sticking to the skin for a minimum of eight hours. As for the best longevity? You’re looking at the Polo Red Ralph Lauren Cologne. Masculine scents also linger, so consider Polo for spicier vibes or Polo Earth for citrusy and aromatic. If you have any doubts, a bit of additional research on fragrance option is all you need to find out if your favorite scent will last.

    Application and Storage Tips for Polo Colognes

    Below are some of the tips to apply your cologne in an effective way:

    Step 1: Moisturize your skin really well to make the perfume linger on your skin for a prolonged time. 

    Step 2: Ensure that you apply cologne to areas of your body that are naturally warm. Also, consider pulse points like the neck, shoulders, and inner elbows.

    Step 3: Hold the bottle 3 inches away.

    Step 4: Do not over-apply your cologne. 

    Step 5: Test its longevity and reapply if needed.

    Step 6: Dab a little onto your pulse points again. 

    Tada, you are good to go!

    Storage Tip 

    Make sure you store your perfume in a dark, cool, and dry place. On the other hand, you can also keep them in their original boxes to let them stay as fresh as new seasons around.

    FAQ's

    Can I wear Polo cologne year-round, or should I switch fragrances with the seasons?

    It depends upon your preference. You can wear Polo cologne year-round as well as switch fragrances with seasons.

    How can I make my Polo cologne last longer throughout the day?

    You can make your Polo cologne last longer throughout the day by applying it on moisturized skin, especially near the pulse points.

    Are there any Polo colognes suitable for sensitive skin or allergies?

    Yes, Polo colognes are suitable for sensitive skin or allergy-prone skin as they are free from alcohol.

    How do I know if a Polo cologne is authentic and not a counterfeit product?

    You can check the overall packaging of the product. If the cellophane is messy or moving around the box, it is a sign that it is a counterfeit product. Another way to tell is by the longevity of the product. Original Polo colognes should last for hours on the skin.

    Are there any Polo colognes that are considered timeless classics?

    Yes, Ralph Lauren Polo Black is one of the colognes that is considered to be among the timeless classics.

    Is Polo red or black better?

    Both of them smell great. For those who love the woody fragrance family, Polo Black is one for you. On the other, Polo Red is for those of you who prefer spraying spicy notes.

    Is Polo Black a summer or winter fragrance?

    Polo Black works well during summer but can also be worn in fall as well as winter.
