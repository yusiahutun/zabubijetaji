Carey Mulligan

Mike Coppola//Getty Images

There were countless stars showcasing chic bobs at the 2024 Oscars ceremony, but a standout was Carey Mulligan's. The impeccable cut was styled with a rounded finish and sleek centre parting reading in equal parts current and classic.

Penelope Cruz

Penelope Cruz recently joined the A-list bob brigade, styling hers with teased-out volume and softly curled ends for a flattering finish.
