Deontay Wilder and Tyson Fury are edging closer to their much-anticipated heavyweight rematch in Las Vegas.

The Gypsy King is looking to dethrone WBC champion Wilder at the MGM Grand Arena, almost 15 months on from their controversial split-decision draw.

Many believe Fury did enough to claim victory in their first encounter, having outboxed the American for the majority of the contest and climbed off the canvas in dramatic fashion late on.

But now the pair will settle their differences once and for all in a pivotal rematch on Saturday night.
