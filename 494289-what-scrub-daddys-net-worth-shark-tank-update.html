What is Scrub Daddy?

It is a company which manufactures various cleaning products. However, the company is primarily known for creating happy faces sponges. It was established in 2012 but gained popularity for its flagship product.

The famous Scrub Daddy sponge is made in a smiley-face shape. It is made from a soft material that is safe for non-stick surfaces. While most sponges are made of polyester, nylon, or cellulose, Scrub Daddy is made of polymer.

This enables it to change in texture in response to different water temperatures. For example, the Scrub Daddy's owner created the sponge to become soft when placed in hot water and harder in cold water.

The company sells the following products:

  • A Scour Daddy
  • A Sponge Daddy
  • A PowerPaste
  • The Scrub Daddy (made in different designs and colours)

Aaron Krause owns the company. He is an accomplished inventor and entrepreneur. He has over two decades of experience in patenting and manufacturing innovative products. He is a bachelor's degree in psychology holder from Syracuse University, where he graduated in 1992.
