Who is Avril Lavigne?

Avril Ramona Lavigne was born in Belleville, Ontario on September 27, 1984 to Jean Claude Lavigne and Judith-Rosanne Loshaw. She is a Canadian singer-songwriter dubbed pop-punk music princess. She has two siblings, Matthew and Michelle, who she claims used to tease her when she sang.

At 16, Avril signed two contracts with Arista Records. Her debut album, Let Go (2002), became the 21st-century best-selling album by a Canadian artist. Her second album, Under My Skin (2004), became her first album to top the Billboard 200 chart in the USA, selling 10 million copies globally.

Avril Lavigne went on to produce several albums, including The Best Thing (2007), Goodbye Lullaby (2011), Avril Lavigne (2013), Head Above Water (2019) and Love Sux (2022). They were all commercial successes and certified gold in Canada, the USA and other countries.
