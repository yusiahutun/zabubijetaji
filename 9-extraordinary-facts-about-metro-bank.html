Metro Bank is the first new high street bank in the UK for over 150 years.

Since its launch in 2010, Metro Bank has revolutionized the traditional banking landscape, opening its doors as the first new high street bank in the UK in over a century and a half. This marked a significant milestone in the banking industry, introducing fresh competition and challenging the established banks.

It offers extended opening hours, including weekends and bank holidays.

Metro Bank understands that banking needs are not limited to traditional working hours. With its customer-centric approach, Metro Bank branches are open seven days a week, allowing customers to access banking services at their convenience. This includes weekends and bank holidays, breaking the norm of limited operating hours.

Metro Bank has a dog-friendly policy in its branches.

Metro Bank is known for its inclusive and pet-friendly approach. In a bid to create a welcoming atmosphere, Metro Bank allows customers to bring their furry friends into the branches. This unique policy has gained widespread attention and appreciation from pet lovers.

It provides instant printing of debit and credit cards in-store.

Gone are the days of waiting anxiously for your new debit or credit card to arrive in the mail. Metro Bank offers an exceptional service – the instant printing of personalized debit and credit cards in-store. This allows customers to receive their cards on the spot, enhancing convenience and reducing wait times.

Metro Bank operates a coin-counting machine in every branch.

Say goodbye to sorting and counting loose change manually. Metro Bank has coin-counting machines available in every branch, allowing customers to easily and accurately count their loose change. This service simplifies the process of depositing coins and makes banking hassle-free.

It offers a 15-minute account opening process.

Opening a new bank account can often be a time-consuming task. However, Metro Bank has streamlined the process with its 15-minute account opening service. By providing all the necessary documentation and fulfilling the requirements, customers can walk away with a fully functional bank account in just 15 minutes.

Metro Bank has a customer service line open 24/7.

It offers fee-free transactions when using your card abroad.

Metro Bank eliminates the hassle of foreign transaction fees when using your card abroad. Whether it’s for shopping, dining, or withdrawing cash, customers can enjoy fee-free transactions in any currency when using their Metro Bank debit or credit cards internationally.

Metro Bank has a strong community involvement through its Metro Bank Magic Money Trees initiative.

Metro Bank is committed to giving back to the community. Through its Metro Bank Magic Money Trees initiative, the bank supports local causes and promotes financial education by providing grants to schools and charities. This initiative showcases Metro Bank’s dedication to making a positive impact in the communities it serves.

Absolutely! Metro Bank provides round-the-clock access to your account through their online and mobile banking platforms. You can perform various transactions, check balances, transfer funds, and even chat with customer service representatives at any time that suits you best.

No, Metro Bank does not charge any additional fees for their extended services. Whether you visit a branch outside of normal banking hours or contact their customer service outside business hours, you won’t incur any extra costs.
