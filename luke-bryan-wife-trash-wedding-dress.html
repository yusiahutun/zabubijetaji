Luke Bryan's Wife Caroline Trashes Wedding Dress

September 27, 2024
blog

The ladies tied to Luke Bryan and Rascal Flatts' Joe Don Rooney were joined by Carah Owen (Randy Owen's daughter-in-law) to trash the dress. That's Caroline Bryan in white, getting sloppy with ribs and ranch dressing while Tiffany Fallon and Owen hose her down with tequila and Budweiser. But they didn't stop there.

It takes a true country girl to go fishing in a wedding gown, but as you can see at Bombs Over Betty's Photography's website, that's exactly what happened. It was B.O.B.'s Jillian Hughes and Tarryn Feldman who called up Bryan and company to do their own "Trash the Dress" shoot. Feldman was unimpressed with other shoots she saw.

"No one felt awkward or strange about rolling around in ranch dressing," she tells the Ram Country Report. To get ready, the ladies picked up a dress from David's Bridal (nope, it's not the actual dress Caroline married Luke in seven years ago), put back a few shots of Fireball Whisky and got crazy with it. Afterward, the gown was donated to a center that re-purposes dresses for other brides.

Luke was on had for some of the photos. The photoshoot went down in March at the Bryan family farm, and Feldman says the women were naturals in front of the camera. That's not a surprise, especially for Fallon, who was the 2005 Playboy Playmate of the Year. Check out all of the fun pics here, and then visit Bombs over Betty Photography on Facebook.
