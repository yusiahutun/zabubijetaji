Where in the world is Taylor Swift? She’s the Kansas City Chiefs’ new number one fan, as determined by the general public. Between swag surfing with Donna Kelce and Brittany Mahomes and delivering expectedly animated fan reactions, Taylor Swift’s 10-second feature every quarter is a fun addition to one of America’s favorite pastimes. She’s also mastered football fan style, so we can’t wait to see what Super Bowl outfit Taylor Swift will wear if she makes it to the big game.

Will Taylor Swift be at the 2024 Super Bowl?

The big question leading up to Super Bowl LVIII is: Will Taylor Swift be there? Fans and critics alike are waiting with bated breath to see if the global superstar will land in Las Vegas on February 11 to support her new beau, Travis Kelce. Right now, we’re still not sure.

This weekend, the Chiefs will play the Baltimore Ravens in the AFC Championship, while the Detroit Lions and the San Francisco 49ers battle it out for the NFC Championship. Should the Chiefs beat the Ravens, they’ll make it to the 2024 Super Bowl. But questions still remain about whether Tay will.

Swift’s upcoming international tour starts on February 7. And she’s headlining four back-to-back shows in Tokyo—which is a 12-hour flight away from Las Vegas—from February 7 to February 10. That leaves a tight window for her to knock out a long flight before resuming her tour in Melbourne on February 16.

Whether or not we get our famed Taylor Swift Super Bowl sighting, her iconic game-day fashion is still something to behold. Her previous game-day looks can help you style your Super Bowl outfit, ensuring you’re the best-dressed person at your Super Bowl party.

  • Taylor Swift at Kansas City Chiefs vs. Miami Dolphins

    One of Taylor Swift’s latest game-day outfits featured a custom Kansas City Chiefs puffer jacket with her boyfriend’s name and number printed on the front, back, and sleeve. In classic Taylor Swift fashion, small business owner Kristin Juszcyk, wife of San Francisco 49ers player Kyle Juszcyk, designed the custom jacket.

  • Taylor Swift at Kansas City Chiefs vs. Cincinnati Bengals

    If I had known letterman jackets would come back in style, I would’ve kept mine from high school!   • Taylor Swift at Kansas City Chiefs vs. Buffalo Bills

    In her “Starlight” era, Swift went vintage for this December 10 game against the Buffalo Bills. She accentuated her oversized crew-neck sweatshirt, found at local Kansas City shop WestsideStorey, with a classic red lip and rings from Mejuri and Mazin Jewels. 

  • Taylor Swift at Kansas City Chiefs vs. Green Bay Packers

    Twinning! The original girl’s girl donned a vibrant red Stella McCartney teddy coat alongside new bestie and wife of Patrick Mahomes, Brittany Mahomes.

  • Taylor Swift at Kansas City Chiefs vs. Los Angeles Chargers

    Swift’s attention to detail never stops. At this Chiefs vs. Chargers game, she accented her Kansas City Chiefs sweatshirt with a custom friendship bracelet with Kelce’s number, 87—a throwback to how the tight end and songstress are connected. 

  • Taylor Swift at Kansas City Chiefs vs. Chicago Bears

    For a game against the Chicago Bears, Swift opted for a simple white top and a Kansas City Chiefs windbreaker. Naturally, she paired the red jacket with a bold red lip.
