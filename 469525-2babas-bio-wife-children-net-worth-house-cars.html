Early life

The personality was born Innocent Ujah Idibia on 18 September 1975 (age 47 as of 2022) in Jos, Plateau, Nigeria. He is the son of Michael Idibia (father) and Rose Idibia (mother). Furthermore, Idibia is the second born of four boys in his family.

Although 2Baba was born in Plateau State, his parents raised him and his brothers (Steven Idibia, Hyacinth Idibia, and Oche Idibia) in Makurdi, the capital of Benue State in central Nigeria.He comes from the Idoma ethnic group. He attended Demonstration Primary School and later joined Mount Saint Gabriel’s Secondary School in Benue State.

After his high school education, 2Baba joined the Institute of Management & Technology, Enugu. He did his preliminary National Diploma course in Business Administration and Management. At college, he started singing and performing at organised shows and events.

He composed songs and did jingles at the GB Fan Club at the Enugu State Broadcasting Services. 2Baba was excellent at music, a factor that forced him to drop out of college and head to Lagos, Nigeria, to pursue his music career.

Professional career

2Baba started his musical career while in college. He then moved to Lagos, Nigeria, in 1997 to perform music and subsequently joined singer Blackface Naija. He then formed a band with Blackface and Faze called Plantashun Boiz. The band released two albums, Body and Soul (2000) and Sold Out (2003).

After their second album, the band disbanded in 2004. However, they later came back together in 2007 to record their final album called Plan B. After disbanding Plantashun Boiz, 2Baba embarked on a solo music career. He released his first solo album in 2004, titled Face 2 Face.

Wife and children

The personality is married and has children. 2Baba’s wife is Annie Macaulay-Idibia. Annie is a Nigerian model, presenter, and actress born on 13 November 1984 (age 37 as of 2022) in Eket, Akwa Ibom, Nigeria. Annie appeared in the music video of 2Baba’s hit song African Queen.

The singer married Annie on 2 May 2012. The couple’s wedding occurred in Lagos, Nigeria, in a private ceremony. 2Baba's children from his marriage to Annie are Olivia Idibia and Isabella Idibia.
