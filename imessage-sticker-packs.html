12 Must-Have iMessage Sticker Packs for Fun Conversations

April 13, 2024
blog

Stickers allow you to add a fun element to your iMessage conversations, and these days it’s not hard to find good stickers. A couple of years ago, we only got them from dedicated sticker packs on the App Store, but nowadays the market has moved towards shipping iMessage stickers alongside your other apps and games.

Our list of the best iMessage stickers reflects that change. We’ve downloaded plenty of great apps with iMessage stickers to save you the hassle of discovering the best of them yourself.

Here’s our list of the best iMessage stickers you can download right now.

1. Reddit

Yes, the official Reddit app ships with some lovely stickers. If you like Reddit’s alien mascot—Snoo—then you’ll love this sticker pack. Apart from the various emotions of Snoo, you get the standard upvote and downvote stickers here.

If these aren’t what you’re looking for, there are a variety of animals, including the good ol’ Doge, that you can use to emote on iMessage.

Download: Reddit for iOS (Free)

2. Nike Run Club

Nike’s popular running app also ships with a few neat stickers. If you feel like you need to send some fitness motivation to others, you can check out these fitness-focused stickers.

You get stickers depicting running, stretching, awards, as well as encouraging stickers such as “Sunday Brunch Earned.” These stickers could be ideal for fitness-minded people.

Download: Nike Run Club for iOS (Free)

3. Apple Fitness

If you have an Apple Watch paired to your iPhone, you will automatically see Apple’s stickers for the Fitness app in the Messages app. We really love the smooth animation on these stickers—a typical Apple touch. You get lots of stickers that offer some motivation to close your move, stand, and exercise rings, which is great to have.

This sticker pack also has dedicated activity-focused stickers for running, walking, swimming, and more workouts that you can track on the Apple Watch. Note that these stickers will show up automatically if you pair an Apple Watch. You can’t download these separately.

The stickers will remain on your iPhone even after you unpair your Apple Watch.

4. Fantastical

Fantastical is one of the best calendar apps on the iPhone, and one of its “hidden” features is a lovely sticker pack. You don’t need to pay anything to use these stickers and they are beautifully designed. You get a smiling calendar buddy in these stickers, and as expected, all the stickers are focused on meetings.

So, if you’re running late, unwell, or just want to schedule a meal with someone, try Fantastical’s neat stickers.

Download: Fantastical for iOS (Free, subscription available)

5. 1Password

At this point, we should clarify that you’re definitely not going through a list of productivity apps on the App Store. This is very much an article where you discover the best stickers for iMessage.

Funnily enough, serious apps such as 1Password have a lighter side buried within iMessage. These stickers feature the various moods of 1Password’s mascot, as well as lots of security-focused messages that you might want to send.

Download: 1Password for iOS (Free, subscription available)

6. Headspace

In the mood for some calming stickers to round off your conversations? Headspace’s soothing stickers will definitely appeal to you. From encouraging you to meditate to wishing you a happy morning, there are a lot of nice choices here.

Download: Headspace for iOS (Free, subscription available)

7. Twitch

While most people use Twitch to watch streamers, you can take that experience to iMessage as well. This is one of the most exhaustive sticker packs we’ve come across on iMessage.

You get all kinds of stickers here, including several popular Twitch emotes. You also have some game-related stickers such as one from The Binding of Isaac. These stickers could help you make iMessage chats look like a stream of Twitch comments.

Download: Twitch for iOS (Free)

8. Horizon Stickers

Enjoy the game Horizon Zero Dawn? Then these stickers will definitely appeal to you. Horizon Stickers is a free download with several stickers featuring the protagonist Aloy.

Related: The Best iPhone Keyboard Apps for Emojis, Emoticons, and Stickers

The stickers are nicely designed, so they’ll be a good fit for those who’ve never played Horizon Zero Dawn too.

Download: Horizon Stickers for iOS (Free)

9. The Last of Us Stickers

Since we’re talking about PlayStation games, please allow us to introduce you to The Last of Us Stickers. This free sticker pack will allow you to bring Ellie stickers to your phone.

A side effect is that some clickers also follow you into this sticker pack, but hey, even your nemesis in the game could be a fun addition to iMessage conversations.

Download: The Last of Us Stickers for iOS (Free)

10. LittleBigPlanet Stickers

No other protagonist from a PlayStation game adds as much fun to iMessage as Sackboy. This is the best of the PlayStation stickers on iMessage and we highly recommend that you check out LittleBigPlanet Stickers.

Sackboy’s cuteness is a good fit for fun conversations with friends and family, so go ahead and have some fun.

Download: LittleBigPlanet Stickers for iOS (Free)

11. Rabbids Stickers

If you enjoyed Rabbids games such as Mario vs Rabbids: Kingdom Battle on the Nintendo Switch, then this is the right sticker pack for you. It features all your favorite Rabbids characters and it does well to capture the craziness of their world.

Download: Rabbids Stickers for iOS (Free)

12. Om Nom Stickers

Fans of the popular iOS game Cut the Rope will enjoy the official sticker pack for the game. The cute protagonist Om Nom features prominently in these stickers.

Om Nom Stickers features lots of cute emotions that strike a chord with young and old people alike, making this ideal for family groups. You can expect favorable reactions from many people with this sticker pack.

Download: Om Nom Stickers for iOS (Free)

An Endless Wave of Stickers

There’s no limit to how many stickers you can download on your iPhone. We’ve barely scratched the surface here because there are so many lovely sticker packs with something for everyone.

However, stickers are just one of the various kinds of iMessage apps. There are several other types of iMessage apps on the App Store that you should definitely explore. From iMessage games to scanner apps, you get all kinds of iMessage apps to check out.
