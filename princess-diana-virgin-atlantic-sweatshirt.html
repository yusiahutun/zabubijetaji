Virgin Atlantic releases modern update on Princess Diana's iconic sweatshirt

May 14, 2024
blog

So many of Princess Diana's most famous outfits are etched in our memories – one of these being the sweatshirt and cycling shorts that she was pictured wearing back in the Nineties, which has been used as a point of reference for casual style ever since. The sweatshirt was Virgin Atlantic merch gifted to her by founder Richard Branson, and the airline is now releasing an updated version of it, as part of the brand's first-ever lifestyle collection.

The 'Fly Virgin Atlantic' sweatshirt that Diana wore became so well known partly because she was pictured in it so many times. According to the royal's personal trainer, Jenni Rivett – who spoke on the topic ahead of its auction in 2019 – Diana would wear it to every single gym session so that photographers were less interested in yet another photo of her in the same look.

"This sweatshirt was given to HRH by Sir Richard Branson," Rivett said. "It became her most worn top on her regular visits to the gym. She felt there were far more important issues to focus on in the world then her gym attire and therefore decided that wearing the famous Virgin sweatshirt to every session, would be a good way to stop the media frenzy."

Of course, the look soon became one of her most memorable off-duty ensembles, and the sweatshirt ended up selling at auction for over £40,000.

Now, Virgin is releasing a new version of the sweatshirt, which has been produced in collaboration with sustainable designers, Fenella Smith and Oliver Co. The 'Queen of the Skies' jumper is heavily inspired by the 'Fly Virgin Atlantic' sweatshirt – it has a very similar motif on the front, and is available in classic navy and cream.

The sweatshirt is part of a wider collection of lifestyle pieces including knitwear, T-shirts and vegan leather accessories. You can shop the entire collection here.
