See &quot;Real Housewives&quot; Star NeNe Leakes' Wedding Dress!

May 29, 2024
blog

Bigger is better!

Check out "Real Housewives of Atlanta" star NeNe Leakes' stunning wedding gown! The reality star's featured in the new issue of Life & Style where she spills details of her $1.8 million big day.

This is the second wedding for NeNe and her hubby Gregg Leakes -- the two divorced in 2011, but continued to stay in contact ... which led to their second trip down the aisle last weekend.

"I feel amazing," she tells the mag. "Both of us want this, so it will definitely work this time. I know exactly what I'm getting myself into."

Talking about her Old Hollywood themed wedding, Leakes tells the mag "It was more romantic this time than the first. Gregg and I are real adults, so it was better."

Leakes wore a $50,000 Baracci gown for the ceremony and a $20,000 Pnina Tornai dress for the reception, which was made up of 400 of the couple's family, closest friends and fellow Bravo stars.

Kim Zolciak, Cynthia Bailey, Phaedra Parks, Patti Stanger, Gretchen Rossi, Vivica A. Fox and Fantasia Barrino were just some of the celebs in attendance -- and everyone dressed the glamorous part!

"I came up with the idea of having an Old Hollywood theme because I thought it would be completely fabulous and everyone would be able to have fun with it," she reveals to the mag. "I just did it my way!"

We'll get to see Leakes' extravagant ceremony play out on TV in "I Dream of NeNe: The Wedding" on Bravo this fall!
