Lady Gabriella Windsor Wears Wedding Dress by Luisa Beccaria at Windsor Castle Wedding

June 30, 2024
blog
  • Lady Gabriella Windsor is marrying Thomas Kingston on May 18, 2019, in St. George's Chapel in Windsor Castle.
  • Members of the royal family are in attendance for the nuptials, including new dad Prince Harry.
  • At her royal wedding, Lady Gabriella wore a stunning white gown which was intricately embroidered and featured a long train.

Following in the footsteps of Meghan Markle and Princess Eugenie, who both got married at Windsor Castle in 2018, Lady Gabriella Windsor has arrived at St. George's Chapel for her nuptials.

Related Story

Lady Gabriella is marrying her longtime partner, Thomas Kingston, who has also dated Pippa Middleton. Arriving at St. George's Chapel by car, the bride ascended the venue's iconic steps alongside her father, Prince Michael of Kent.

Immediately evident was the incredible train on Lady Gabriella's dress, which is by Italian designer Luisa Beccaria.

Chris Jackson//Getty Images

Chris Jackson//Getty Images

The dreamy Luisa Beccaria wedding dress, which was created especially for the bride, featured long sleeves made of sheer lace, a fitted bodice, and a long veil made of white and pink tulle. The bride carried a stunning bouquet of pink and white flowers, and wore a diamond tiara.

Per the royal family's website, "Lady Gabriella wore a Russian Fringe style diamond tiara also worn by her grandmother, HRH Princess Marina, Duchess of Kent, and her mother, HRH Princess Michael of Kent, on their wedding days."

Lady Gabriella's gown featured intricate embroidery, making the bride look flawless:

CHRIS JACKSON//Getty Images

Speaking to The Independent about creating the dream wedding gown, Luisa Beccaria revealed, "Ella is very independent type of person." She continued, "She doesn't listen and say what her parents say." Of Lady Gabriella's dress, the royal family's official website revealed, "Its blush shade came from layers of tulle and organdie beneath the lace, which created the body of the gown."

The groom, Kingston, was also spotted arriving at the venue looking dapper in a suit with a pink tie:
