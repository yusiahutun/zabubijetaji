Married Missouri high school teacher Brianna Coppage, 28, put on leave after students discovered her

July 28, 2024
blog

A married mother and high school teacher has been suspended from her post after students discovered her OnlyFans page.

Brianna Coppage, 28, was put on leave from St Clair High School in Missouri after administrators received a report an employee 'may have posted inappropriate media on one or more internet sites'.

The English teacher, who used the alias Brooklin Love for her pornographic content, was suspended on Wednesday.

She told STL Today she always knew 'the day was coming' when her dual life would be exposed but has 'no regret'.

She said:  'It was kind of always like this cloud hanging over my head, like I never knew when I would be discovered.

Brianna Coppage had been teaching at St Clair High School for two years before her double life as an OnlyFans performer was revealed

Coppage used the alias Brooklin Love for her content which she put online through X (formerly Twitter) and OnlyFans where subscribers could access it for $10 per month

'Then, about two weeks ago, my husband and I were told that people were finding out about it. So I knew this day was coming.' 

Coppage believes she was identified after her husband appeared in one of her videos and someone tipped off the local school board.

A post on her X account, formerly Twitter, says: 'Y’all are wild, someone shared my videos with my entire hometown. I hope you enjoyed watching.'

All users of OnlyFans must be at least 18 according to the site's terms of service. 

But while there are stringent age verification checks on creators, the platform only says that subscribers or fans 'may have to provide various personally identifying information' depending on their country of residence.

The mom-of-two joined adult subscription site OnlyFans to supplement her $42,000 a year teaching job in the summer.

She revealed she makes up to an additional $10,000 per month from her raunchy performances, which fans can subscribe to for $10 per month.

Her X page, where she refers to herself as 'Your favorite Milf' ,states she is among the top four per cent of creators. 

However she admitted is she was 'probably never going to teach again' after her activities were discovered. 

Coppage added: 'That was kind of the risk I knew I was taking. I am sad about that. I do miss my students.'

Coppage, pictured with her husband Phillip, believes her identity may have been revealed after she and her spouse both starred in a video together

Brianna Coppage was identified as the performer behind the Brooklin Love OnlyFans page where subscribers can pay to watch adult content

But she stopped short of expressing any regret and said: 'I know it can be taboo, or some people may believe that it is shameful, but I don’t think sex work has to be shameful. I do just wish things just happened in a different way.'

Coppage had been teaching at St Clair High School, near St Louis, for two years but insisted none of her content was filmed on school grounds and that her porn career was 'completely separate'.

She said she doesn't disagree that teachers should be 'moral leaders' but maintained that she was not doing anything illegal and is a 'good friend' and 'good family member'.

Coppage added: 'I taught the curriculum. I taught students reading and writing, and I didn’t guide them on my thoughts or beliefs. … And I can’t control what people think of me.'

A statement from the school's superintendent Kyle Kruse said: 'The district has engaged legal counsel to conduct a comprehensive investigation into this matter.

 'Actions taken as a result of the investigation will be in accordance with board policy and with guidance from legal counsel.'
