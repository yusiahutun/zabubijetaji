Known for putting on glamorous showings whenever she attends a red carpet event, on her days out of the spotlight Naomi Watts is a fan of dressing down.

Seen filling up her tank at a gas station in Santa Monica, California on Tuesday, Naomi was spot on trend as she rocked a dotted shirt.

However the 44-year-old suffered a slight fashion faux pas as the pink bra she wore underneath her white blouse was highly visible.

Spot on trend: Naomi Watts was seen wearing a dotted shirt on Tuesday in Santa Monica, California

Slight overexposure: Naomi's pink bra was highly visible through her sheer blouse

Naomi teamed the loosely fitted mullet style shirt with a pair of lavender grey skinny jeans and strapped sandals.

Hiding her eyes behind Chanel sunglasses, Watt appeared to be enjoying a make-up free day.

The Movie 43 actress wore her short loosely waved blonde hair down in a centre parting as she raised a smile for photographers before hopping back in her 4x4.

Dressed down: The 44-year-old actress teamed her shirt with a pair of dark lavender jeans

On the go: Naomi wore her blonde hair down in a centre parting as she hid her eyes behind aviator shades

Keeping it causal: Watts ditched her heels as she hopped into her car wearing a pair of sandals

In a good mood: The Movie 43 actress flashed a smile at photographers

Following the end of the busy award season, it will be back to work for Naomi as she has several films in the works.

Fans of the Kent born star can look forward to seeing her take on the role of the late Princess Diana in the Oliver Hirschbiegel directed biopic Diana.

And currently enjoying a break from the demands of being an actress, Naomi has been spending her time with her sons Alexander and Samuel Kai, and her partner Liev Schreiber.

Make-up free and loving it! The stunning actress showcased her flawless complexion

Time off work: Following the come down of the award season Naomi has been taking life easy

The actress recently told The Hollywood Reporter: '[Being a mother is] pretty exhausting. It's so great, it's the best thing. Most high rewards you can have in anything, but it's definitely exhausting being a mom.

'I do my own laundry, but I sometimes have help. I cook. I definitely don't have a chef.

'People see me as the dolled-up movie star type on the red carpet, but in real life, I don't look anything like that. I blend easily into a crowd.'
