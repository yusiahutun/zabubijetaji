Does Anuel AA have a new girlfriend and will he stop hitting on Karol G?

June 21, 2024
blog

Anuel AA surprised his followers on social media by sharing an image of himself kissing a woman.

The rapper posted a photo on his Instagram stories showing him being very romantic with the young woman, whom he grabs by the bottom while she grabs his face with one hand. Both appear to have their eyes closed, enjoying the passionate moment.

This publication of the singer of "Mas rica que ayer" coincided with the 21st birthday of his ex-partner, Yailin La Mas Viral.

The Dominican rapper spent the date in a hospital bed, recovering from cosmetic procedures, in the company of her friend Tekashi 6ix9ine, who showered her with expensive and luxurious gifts.

Is Anuel AA debuting new love or is it publicity for a new song?

The rapper's photo with the curvaceous young woman raised doubts among his fans, who are unsure whether the Puerto Rican has decided to give himself a new chance at love or whether it is a merely publicity stunt for a new song.

Anuel AA announced in February that he had split from Yailin while she was pregnant. After the birth of their daughter Cattleya, the two confirmed the split officially.

After that, the singer dedicated himself to posting messages that Karol G was still the owner of his heart, even dedicating the song "Mejor que yo" to her, but now the Colombian singer is happily in a relationship with Feid.

Even at an awards ceremony, Anuel AA wore a t-shirt with the image of the song "Secreto", which he made with Karol G, and with a message that read: "You're with FEID but you know you're mine".

Could it be that the Puerto Rican has finally given up on the singer and found love again in the arms of the young woman he showed off on his social networks?
