Black Clover is a popular anime series featuring many female characters with unique magical powers and abilities. While the series contains incredible male characters, female Black Clover characters are equally impressive. Black Clover is a popular manga and anime series that has won the hearts of many fans. The series' plot revolves around the adventures of Asta. However, it also articulates the stories of several impressive women with diverse backgrounds and personalities.

Female Black Clover characters

1. Noelle Silva

Noelle Silva is undoubtedly the best girl in Black Clover. She is a member of the Black Bulls and comes from the powerful Silva family. Noelle is known for her amazing water magic and is often seen as a central figure in the series.

She also provides emotional and physical support for Asta and the rest of the team. Fans love Noelle for her cute personality, love for Asta, and determination to prove herself worthy of her family's name.

2. Mereoleona Vermilion

Mereoleona Vermilion is a hot-tempered and fierce female Black Clover character born to the House Vermilion. She is the Crimson Lion Magic Knight squad captain and uses magic powers to generate and manipulate fire, shaping it into a lion's paw. Her immense strength and physical abilities make her a fan favourite.

3. Secre Swallowtail

Secre Swallowtail is a Black Bull squad member of the Magic Knights and former servant of Prince Lemiel Silvamilion. She has become one of the series' best girls, charming Noelle despite her human and bird form. She also possesses powerful sealing magic attributes that aid her in opening and closing spells and is extremely loyal to Astra.

4. Mimosa Vermilion

Mimosa Vermillion is a member of the Clover Kingdom's noble family and a cousin of the Vermillion family. She is a kind-hearted girl who cares deeply for her friends and is always willing to help them in any way she can.

She uses plant magic and is often seen as a healer nursing her teammates' injuries. Mimosa is also among the girls who love Asta and openly express her affection for him.

5. Charmy Pappitson

Charmy Pappitson is a Black Bull member known for her magical food-creation abilities. She is a gluttonous girl who loves to eat, especially sweets. Charmy is often seen as a comical character in the series, but she can be serious when the situation demands it. Fans love Charmy for her cute appearance and her quirky personality.

6. Vanessa Enoteca

Vanessa Enoteca is a member of the Black Bulls, known for her curse magic and love for drinking. She is dressed seductively and has a high affinity for handsome men. However, despite her casual, relaxed persona, Vanessa is always ready to guide and support her teammates when they need it the most. Fans love Vanessa for her charming personality and ever-ready timing to offer drinks to any of her friends.

7. Vanica Zogratis

Vanica Zogratis is a member of the Dark Triad and among the strongest antagonist devils. Her cynical demeanour won the hearts of many fans after her introduction to the Black Vover Manga.

She despises idleness and has a hobby of battling heavyweight opponents like Lolopechka to pass the time. Vanica has a barbaric personality and often mocks weaker opponents referring to them as garbage.

8. Dorothy Unsworth

Dorothy Unsworth is the Captain of the Coral Peacock and is known for her dream magic. She often appears as a gag character who is always asleep. Her array of magical powers allows for the manifestation and manipulation of a dream-like world. Fans love Dorothy for her enigmatic, eccentric personality and powerful magical abilities.

9. Charlotte Roselei

Charlotte Roselei is the Captain of the Blue Rose Knights and is known for her powerful plant magic. She is a strong and confident leader who will do anything to protect her friends and family. Fans love Charlotte for her leadership skills and her impressive magical abilities. Although she dislikes many men, she is nice to Yami Sukehiro, whom she secretly loves.

10. Sister Lily

Sister Lily Aquaria works as a nun in a Hage orphanage. She is well-known for her kindness and strong faith in the gods of the Clover Kingdom. She rejects Asta's marriage proposal and is abducted by Lily to demonstrate his Soul Magic. Despite her character's sinister turn, fans still adore Sister Lily for her gentle nature, dedication to helping others, and her positive influence on Asta.

11. Sol Marron

Sol Marron is a former Eye of the Midnight Sun member known for her steel magic. She is a skilled fighter who uses magical abilities to manipulate metal and create powerful attacks. Although she is a feminist and despises men, fans love Sol for her fierce personality and impressive fighting skills.

12. Rebecca Scarlet

Rebecca Scarlet is a member of the Crimson Lion Kings and is known for her gravity magic. She is a powerful mage who can manipulate gravity to create powerful attacks. Fans love Rebecca for her cool-headed personality, impressive magical skill, and profound love for Astra.

13. Kahono

Kahono is a member of the Seabed Temple and is known for her sound magic. She is a talented singer who can use her voice to control water and create powerful attacks. Fans love Kahono for her beautiful voice and her kind-hearted nature.

14. Mariella

Mariella is a powerful elf once a member of the Eye of the Midnight Sun. Her beauty, calm personality, and elegant style make her one of the most popular female Black Cover characters. Mariella is a straightforward personality and former assassin who believes saving others can atone for the blood in her hands. Her redemption arc and kindness have also made her a fan favourite.

15. Sally

Sally is a former Eye of the Midnight Sun member known for her creation magic. She is a genius inventor who creates various magical beasts and tools to aid her in battle. Fans love Sally for her intelligence, loyalty and her unique supernatural abilities.

16. Grey

Grey is a member of the Black Bulls and is known for her transformation magic. After her parents died, Grey was raised by her grandfather, who mistreated her, making her feel abandoned.

She ran away from home and met rebel Julius, who protected her until the Captain of the Guard killed him. Although she was sentenced to execution, Astra and Yuno saved her. After that, she joined the Black Bulls as a medic, and their safety is paramount to anything else.

17. Fana

Fana is a pink-haired girl and a member of the Eye of the Midnight Sun known for her powerful fire magic. She is a fierce fighter who can create powerful explosions and control flames. Fana is a fan-favourite owing to her unmatched strength, loyalty and determination to protect family and friends at any cost making her a true hero.

18. Acier Silva

Acier Silva is a stubbornly independent woman who disobeys commands and prefers things done her way. She is loyal to those she cares about, and her sharp tongue enables her to get out of even the stickiest situations. Acier has countlessly helped her friends out in a time of danger from the strongest opposition serving in their best interest.

19. Witch Queen

The Witch Queen is the supreme ruler of the Witches Realm and the series' main female Black Clover antagonist. She is a skilled hand-to-hand combatant with great magical power, allowing her to cast spells.

Her spells include Levitation, Teleportation, and Telekinesis, making her a formidable opponent and force to reckon with. Despite her cold demeanour, she has a soft spot for children and adores them.

20. Licita

Black Clover's Licita is an orphaned girl raised by the Wizard King and becomes a powerful wizard. Licita is a rare magical creature with a mixture of kind, gentle, fierce, and temperamental emotions.

Licita's formidable wizardry wields her immense power to grant her and others numerous wishes. Licita is a fan favourite despite abandoning her children due to her selflessness.
