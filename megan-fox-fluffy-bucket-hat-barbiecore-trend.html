Megan Fox keeps the Barbiecore trend alive in this season's must-have accessory

June 20, 2024
blog

The actress made a vibrant statement with her fluffy fuchsia bucket hat

    Intermittent Instagram poster Megan Fox has never been one for understated – and we love her all the more for it. Whenever the actress indulges her 20.7m Instagram followers, sharing a new, seriously glamorous, insight into her wardrobe game, we can't help but pay attention.

    MORE: Megan Fox and Machine Gun Kelly channel ‘Y2K at Christmas’

    Megan's eclectic sense of style, perhaps best described as 'Y2K-sultry-meets-high-octane-glam', caught our eye recently as the 36-year-old cemented her penchant for a fluffy bucket hat. In fact, eagled-eyed Megan fans will have spotted that the playful style has made up a major part of her accessory repertoire this year.

    A quick scroll through the star's Instagram grid proves that she is something of a fluffy bucket pioneer, having hopped on the hat trend right at the start of 2022 with a camouflage-inspired piece that accompanied her on a trip to Lake Como. Burgundy and deep purple are a couple of other shades that she has rocked this year, but it's fair to say that her latest version was a cut above the rest.

    In a post shared on Instagram, the actress proved that the TikTok-adored Barbiecore trend is going absolutely nowhere. Megan was serving 2000s It-girl in a fuchsia-coloured fluffy bucket which added a vibrant splash of pink to her sculpted halter dress. Look out, Margot Robbie.
