Biography

Taimak was born in Los Angeles, CA on June 27, 1964. Actor, martial artist and stuntman most famous for his role as Leroy Green in the 1985 martial arts musical The Last Dragon. He has also appeared in music videos like Janet Jackson‘s “Let’s Wait Awhile” and received black belts in a variety of disciplines, including Goju Ryu Karate, Jujitsu, and Tae Kwon Do. He appeared with actor Courtney Gains in the 1999 film Dreamers. He was raised in New York City until age 7, when he and his family moved to Europe. At age 11 he met legendary Italian director Federico Fellini, who took him on a tour of the set for the film Roma.
