Link Neal biography and early life

Link Neal was born on 1st June 1978 at a place called Boone Trail in North Carolina. His mother’s name is Sue Capps while that of his father is Charles Lincoln Neal II. At the age of two years, his parents divorced. His mother got married to Jimmy Capps, her high school sweetheart. They relocated to Buies Creek.

For his early childhood education, he attended Buies Creek Elementary School before proceeding to Harnett Central High School for his high school education. After graduating from high school, he got enrolled at North Carolina State University. Talking about Link Neal family, you will be surprised to know that he does not have any siblings. Good Morning Mythical channel

The Good Morning Mythical Channel was established by Link Neal together with his childhood friend Rhett McLaughlin. The duo met in the first grade, and they schooled together until university where they graduated with engineering degrees at the North Carolina State University. You will be surprised to know that they both quit their engineering jobs to focus on the YouTube channel.

In January 2011, they started a morning that was known as “Good Morning Chia Lincoln.” The show became very popular on YouTube. They relocated to Los Angeles in search for a bigger audience. After relocating to Los Angeles, they changed the name of the show to “Good Mythical Morning.” This show usually airs every Monday to Friday at 6.00 am EST.

As the hosts of the show, Link and Rhett usually do crazy things to entertain their fans. They explore surprising new products, eat truly unbelievable things, implement serious experiments in hilarious ways, compete in original games with celebrity guests, and so much more.
