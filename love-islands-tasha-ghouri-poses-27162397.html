Love Island's Tasha Ghouri poses in lace underwear ahead of ITV show debut

January 30, 2024
blog

Love Island contestant Tasha Ghouri has stunned fans with a new snap that shows off the star’s impressive figure as she posed in lace underwear.

The islander, who is set to join her fellow contestants in the villa tonight (6 June) at 9pm, has uploaded a new picture to her Instagram profile ahead of her appearance on ITV2’s Love Island.

Tasha joins the latest series as the show’s first deaf contestant and fans are already backing the star, way before she makes her television debut.

Ghouri posed on a white chair wearing a pair of white denim shorts as she brought her hand up to her chin.

She wore a purple lace bra with a matching blazer as she flashed a smile for the camera, her blonde hair slicked back and her bronzed glow shining through.

The star wore minimal makeup that highlighted her natural beauty and was accessorised with a dainty chain necklace.

The islanders are in Mallorca and settling in ahead of the first episode so their social media accounts are being run by their friends and family.

In the caption of her post, the person running her account wrote: “Oh my goodness! Who is excited? We are! Today is the day our baby enters the villa to find the one, show off her dance moves and to wear her superpower with pride! Tune in at 9pm!”

They signed off with a palm tree, heart, dance and ear emoji.

They also included the hashtags “love island,” “team Tasha” and “Tasha Ghouri”.

Fans went wild for the contestant and rushed to leave comments of praise for the picture.

Underwear brand Boux Avenue called her “dreamy” while the official Boots UK Instagram account commented that it was “not long now” until the show airs.

A kind follower called her “so pretty” and another left: “Can’t wait to watch you, beaut!”

One eager fan said: “Hope the personality matches the looks!”

Tasha has a big love for dancing and often posts videos of herself popping some moves on her Instagram profile.

In the previous post, the “dancing queen” showed off her best moves in a dance studio wearing an all-black outfit.

Her defined abs were revealed by the minuscule crop top and low waisted cargo trousers as she performed a street-style routine.
