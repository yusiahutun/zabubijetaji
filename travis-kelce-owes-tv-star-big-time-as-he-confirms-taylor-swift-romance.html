Travis Kelce 'owes TV star big time' as he 'confirms' Taylor Swift romance

February 17, 2024
blog

Travis dropped a major hint to Swifties and they are definitely listening. The American footballer thanked two TV stars who claimed they were the ones to convince Taylor Swift to give Travis Kelce a chance – and that’s all the confirmation fans needed!

After the Taylor Swift and Matty Healy saga ended in June, fans of the music icon were eager to know who will be the Lover crooner’s new romantic rendezvous partner. While Taylor Swift is yet to say anything official, her attendance at two Kansas City Chiefs matches with a celebrity-studded guest list is giving hope to fans that ‘Traylor’ could be confirmed soon. Now, Travis Kelce has added himself into the mix with a very questionable comment.

Travis Kelce seems to confirm Taylor Swift romance

Travis appears to show he’s not one to keep things under wraps and Swifties are loving it. While the two parties are yet to make anything official, the NFL heartthrob’s latest Instagram comment is ‘enough’ to confirm the news.

TV host Charissa Thompson and Sports commentator Erin Andrews urged Taylor Swift to go on a date with Kelce on their podcast Calm Down back on August 3. This was weekes before she was spotted at the game and sending everyone into a frenzy.

Both Charissa and Erin boasted about their ‘friend’ Travis and asked Taylor to go on a date with him ‘for America.’

The duo said: “Do it for yourself, do it for us and do it for the people. Because no one will give you a better time than this guy.”

The celebrities reshared a clip of the August podcast on October 2 to Instagram and tagged both Tay Tay and Trav. To fans’ surprise, the Kansas City Chiefs Tight End responded to the video himself – this is the part you need to pay attention to.

He wrote: “You two are something else!! I owe you big time!!”

Reports reveal Traylor’s secret date

In between speculations of a Traylor romance – the celebrity couple name fans have already dubbed the pair – more rumors have spilled over. Sources close to the duo revealed Taylor went on a secret date with Travis following his birthday on October 5th. But they made sure they avoided the press to not get any attention.

The source reportedly claimed Taylor flew from Nashville to Kansas City a day after the 34th birthday of the sports legend.

Days after his birthday, Kelce also faced the press and even answered questions about his personal life. Speaking of the extra attention that he has been getting thanks to the dating rumors, he said: “You’ve got a lot of people that care about Taylor, and for good reason.

“You just have to keep living, learning and enjoying the moments. At the end of the day, I have always been good at compartmentalizing and being able to stay focused. I will just keep rolling with that.”
