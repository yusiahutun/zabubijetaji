One Punch Man is a popular anime and manga series that tells the story of Saitama, a seemingly ordinary man who has become a superhero. Saitama, the series' protagonist, has immense power and can destroy any foe with just one punch despite his plain appearance, lack of a flashy costume, or superhero identity. Apart from the main male character, many female One Punch Man characters have made the series endearing, fun, and exciting.

Female One Punch Man characters are fascinating because they break away from traditional gender roles often found in superhero stories. The female characters in One Punch Man are strong, capable, and often hold positions of power.
