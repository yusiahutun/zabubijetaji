What to Do If You Forgot Your Disney+ Password

June 02, 2024
blog

Disney+ has hundreds of TV shows and films for you to watch, with more added regularly. The app is responsive and allows you to customize your account settings and profiles with ease.

If you forget your Disney+ password, be assured that you can still get back into your account by following a few steps.

Send a New Disney+ Password to Your Email Address

The simplest thing to do if you have forgotten your password is try to log in, then, when you're at the stage of needing to enter your password, click Forgot Password.

You'll be emailed to the email address you registered to Disney+ with to reset your password. Simply open the email and follow the instructions to create a new password.

Managing a lot of passwords can be difficult if you have different ones for different platforms (which is recommended for security).

While it's most likely that you forgot your password if it's suddenly not working, perhaps you're worried about someone hacking your account. In which case, changing your email address and password on Disney+ will ensure it's secure and put your mind at rest.

What to Do if You Can’t Access Your Email Address

Changing your email address on Disney+ is very simple and can be done on either the web or mobile app, depending on your device.

How to Change Your Email Address on Disney+ (Browser)

To change your Disney+ account's email address on the web, do the following:

  • Go to Disney+ and log in.
  • Hover over your profile icon and click Settings.
  • On the Account settings page, click over the Edit icon next to your email address.
  • You'll receive a verification code to your registered email address. Enter that code into Disney+ and click Continue.
  • Enter your desired new email address, and then click Save. You can also check the box to Log out of all devices if you wish.

    • You should have now successfully changed your email address on Disney+.

    How to Change Your Email Address on Disney+ (Mobile)

    To change your Disney+ email address on the mobile app, do the following:

  • Open the Disney+ app and select a profile.
  • Tap your profile icon in the bottom right corner.
  • Tap Account.
  • On the Account page, tap Change next to your registered email address.
  • You'll receive a verification code in your inbox. Put that code into Disney+ and tap Continue.
  • Type in your new email and then tap Save. You can also tap Log out of all devices if you wish to do so.
