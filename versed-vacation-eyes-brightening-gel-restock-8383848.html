The $20 Eye Gel That Tightens&quot; Skin Is Back After Selling Out 3 Times

February 20, 2024
blog

When a product repeatedly sells out and has shoppers anxiously awaiting its return, we’re immediately intrigued. Especially when it’s an eye treatment that has reviewers gushing over how well it’s diminished their dark circles and smoothed their fine lines. 

Versed, a skincare brand that offers affordable formulas with proven ingredients and has celebrity fans like Cameron Diaz, has gone viral for several products, including its skincare headband (it is, in fact, worth the hype) and the Vacation Eyes Brightening Eye Gel. The lightweight, brightening treatment has shoppers flooding the reviews section with an abundance of ‘when will it come back’ inquiries.

We’re here to deliver some good news: It’s finally back in stock after selling out on three separate occasions, but based on its popularity, we have an inkling it won’t last long.   

Versed Vacation Eyes Brightening Eye Gel

On TikTok alone, the product has over four million views, with users praising the refreshing gel’s cooling effect as a soothing first step of their pre-makeup regimen. The formula combines vitamin C, coffee extract, ginseng root extract, and niacinamide, which work together to improve dark circles and minimize puffiness. Aside from placing on the under-eye area, it can also be used on the eyelids as a makeup primer to combat oil. The brand recommends letting the gel absorb for about a minute before continuing with your makeup application. 

To reap the eye treatment’s benefits, the brand recommends gently tapping a small dot above and below the eyes in addition to your temples in the morning and at night. If you’re unsure where to fit the eye gel into your routine, the brand advises using it after cleansing your skin and applying serum but before putting on your moisturizer. 

Shoppers who have snatched up the thrice-sold-out gel keep coming back for more and are likely to grab it again now that it’s back in stock. One reviewer who’s tried their fair share of expensive eye treatments says nothing has “worked this well and this fast” to “brighten and tighten” their eye area. Another person called it the “perfect base” for wearing under concealer since it “wakes up [their] eyes instantly.” A third customer agreed, saying their “concealer won’t budge” when layered on top. 

Head over to Versed’s website to snag your own tube of Vacation Eyes while it’s still in stock, and keep scrolling for other shopper-loved skincare favorites.
