She is the latest addition to the Kardashian clan and the mother of the newest Kardashian in town, so there is no doubt that this is a hot topic. Before we delve into the size of Blac Chyna and other body statistics, here is some background information about the vixen herself.

If it was not immediately obvious that ‘Blac Chyna’ is a nickname, then there is a problem. Either way, her real name is Angela Renée White and she was born on 11 May 1988 as the daughter of Shalana Jones-Hunter and Eric Holland. However, her alias was not always Blac Chyna, because back then (2010) she was stripping under the nickname “Cream”. She got her current name from a regular guest in a strip club.

The man called himself “Black China” because he was a black man with Chinese-looking eyes and she could identify with him. Later that year, Chyna posed for the cover of Dimepiece magazine, and it was the first of many upcoming magazine covers. In the same year, she began appearing in music videos, which earned her the title “Video Fairy”.

Today she is a proud entrepreneur and owner of an online boutique ’88fin’ and a beauty bar ‘LASHED’. Unfortunately, she’s more popular with the Kardashians for her dedication than for her achievements, but we bet that’s still good for business.

Before we delve too deeply into her background story, let’s start with this topic and start with Blac Chyna’s height.
