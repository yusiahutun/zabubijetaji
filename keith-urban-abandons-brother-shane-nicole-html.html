Keith Urban 'abandons' his brother Shane because of Nicole

He's got a $75 million fortune, a $2.7 million car and a wife who's a Hollywood star.

But it seems not all of country music singer Keith Urban's family is living the high life.

New Idea claims the 49-year-old Australian superstar - who with wife Nicole Kidman forges one of the world's top celebrity couples - has 'abandoned' his older brother Shane, a single father-of-three from Queensland.
