David Harbour and Lily Allen couldn't contain their holiday cheer on Tuesday night when they attended the premiere of his dark holiday comedy Violent Night.

The 47-year-old Stranger Things star and the 37-year-old singer–songwriter cozied up together on the red carpet at the iconic Chinese Theatre in Hollywood.
