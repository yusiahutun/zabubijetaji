Eva Thomas is the Senior Commerce Writer at InStyle, where she's the driving force behind all things fashion, beauty, and celebrity. With a knack for infusing her own stories with a fresh, personal voice, Eva's writing is as informative as it is enjoyable and fun to read. Picture her as your chic, in-the-know BFF, ready to dish out advice on anything and everything. In a crowded landscape, Eva's conversational writing style stands out; she loves letting readers into her life.

Experience

Eva Thomas is a seasoned fashion journalist with over 6 years of experience in the industry. Her journey began in 2017 as a fashion news writer at Who What Wear, where she quickly established herself as a rising talent. In 2018, Eva further expanded her expertise as a celebrity news writer at The Zoe Report, mastering the fast-paced environment of a newsroom while delving deeper into the realms of celebrity, fashion, and beauty.

In 2019, Eva's career reached new heights when she secured her dream role at InStyle and relocated to New York City to pursue her childhood dreams. At InStyle, Eva immersed herself in the world of commerce, transforming her love of shopping and her knack for finding the best deals into a professional pursuit. With an uncanny ability to spot the latest trends and track down celebrity styles in record time, she quickly became the go-to authority on all things celebrity fashion and beauty.

Today, as the Senior Commerce Writer at InStyle, Eva is at the forefront of the fashion and beauty scene, pitching, writing, and strategizing about the latest trends and must-have products. Her dedication, expertise, and passion shine through in every piece she produces, making her a trusted source for readers seeking insider knowledge and inspiration.
