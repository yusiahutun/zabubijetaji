Outstanding African wedding attire for couples

Lately, a lot of Africans are reverting to a traditional look for their wedding as more people embrace their heritage. Typically, a majority of Africans wed in a church with the bride in a white wedding dress and the bridegroom in a suit. Some Africans, such as Nigerians, opt for a traditional wedding or both with the former holding more weight in any wedding-related discussion.
