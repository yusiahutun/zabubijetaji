FAQs

1. What is the Xterra World Tour?

The Xterra World Tour is an off-road triathlon series consisting of races held in various locations worldwide.

2. What distances are covered in the Xterra races?

The Xterra races typically consist of a 1.5-kilometer swim, a 30-kilometer mountain bike ride, and a 10-kilometer trail run.

3. Can anyone participate in the Xterra World Tour?

Yes, anyone with a passion for off-road racing can participate in the Xterra World Tour. There are age group categories for all skill levels.

4. Are there qualifying races for the Xterra World Championship?

Yes, there are Xterra races designated as “Gold” events, which offer qualifying spots for the Xterra World Championship.

5. Are the Xterra races suitable for beginners?

The Xterra races can be physically demanding, so it is recommended that beginners have some experience in swimming, cycling, and running before participating.

6. What makes the Xterra World Tour unique?

The Xterra World Tour stands out for its challenging off-road courses that take athletes through breathtaking landscapes, such as forests, mountains, beaches, and deserts.

7. How many races are included in the Xterra World Tour?

The Xterra World Tour typically includes over 30 races in various countries around the world.

8. Is there prize money awarded to winners?

Yes, there is prize money awarded to the top finishers in the Xterra World Championship race.

9. Can spectators attend Xterra races?

Yes, spectators are welcome at Xterra races. They can cheer on the athletes, enjoy the festival-like atmosphere, and explore the surrounding areas.

10. How can I get involved in the Xterra World Tour?

To get involved in the Xterra World Tour, you can visit their official website to find upcoming races, register to participate, and learn more about the event.
