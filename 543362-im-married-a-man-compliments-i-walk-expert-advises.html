Problem started with inability to provide

Counsellor Jeniffer Kiarie explained that whatever is happening in the woman's life is common in households where the man can no longer provide.

According to her, the man's ego is hurt because the woman has taken up the role of the lead provider.

"It may not even be malicious, which could explain why he walks away silently whenever she tells him of her discomfort with the current arrangement and the emotional neglect she is experiencing," she started.

Kiarie said that it is hard for a man to be emotionally present for his woman when he cannot take care of her and the family financially.

Wife feels she has male energy

On the other hand, this woman subconsciously detects the male energy she has adopted by taking over the lead provider role.

That is why she craves compliments and emotional support from her man so she can feel the opposite: what it feels like to be a woman.

"Instead of putting more pressure on the man, now that he is already experiencing it, she should support him in coming up with an income-generating activity," Kiarie advised.

She reiterated that such support doesn't have to be financial, as emotional support would be helpful.

What is the way forward?

However, it is essential to recall that the lady is feeling depressed, so she may currently not be in a position to support him.

In this regard, she needs to seek emotional support from a non-judgmental and caring person who will not mislead her into leaving the man.

"Once she is emotionally well, let her work with her man in addressing the prevailing challenge," she continued.

Kiarie pointed out that the man in question doesn't seem to have another problem apart from the joblessness and the impact on his ego.

This is because those with underlying self-esteem issues become insecure and may abuse the wife emotionally, something he is not doing.
