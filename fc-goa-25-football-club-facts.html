FC Goa was established in 2014.

Ever since its inception in 2014, FC Goa has been making waves in the Indian football scene. With their passion, determination, and talented players, they have quickly become one of the most popular football clubs in India.

FC Goa is based in the state of Goa.

As the name suggests, FC Goa is located in the mesmerizing state of Goa, known for its beautiful beaches, vibrant culture, and love for the beautiful game. The club proudly represents the spirit and enthusiasm of the Goan people.

FC Goa has a passionate fanbase.

The fans of FC Goa, known as the Gaurs, are some of the most passionate and dedicated supporters in Indian football. They cheer for the team with unwavering loyalty, turning the stadium into a sea of orange during every match.

FC Goa has consistently qualified for the ISL playoffs.

FC Goa’s home stadium is the Jawaharlal Nehru Stadium.

The iconic Jawaharlal Nehru Stadium in Fatorda, Goa, serves as the home ground for FC Goa. This state-of-the-art stadium provides a fantastic atmosphere for both players and fans alike, as they come together to witness thrilling matches.
