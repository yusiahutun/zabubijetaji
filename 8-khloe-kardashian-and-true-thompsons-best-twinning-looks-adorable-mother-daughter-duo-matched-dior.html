Let’s take a look at eight of the mother and daughter duo’s best matching outfits to date.

1. Italian perfection in Dolce & Gabbana

The mother-daughter duo took Italy by storm during their recent holiday, seemingly channeling the “tomato girls” aesthetic in matching floral Dolce & Gabbana dresses.

Khloé captioned the Instagram post, “L’amor che move il sol e l’altre stelle” which means “the love that moves the sun and the other stars”.

Even big sister Kim Kardashian gave her seal of approval, commenting, “This is the cutest picture I’ve ever seen.”

2. Christmas cuties

Kardashian and Thompson celebrated Christmas 2022 in style courtesy of these custom red dresses from designer Nicolas Jebran.

For Christmas 2019, Kardashian kitted her daughter out in gold to match her sparkling frock from Los Angeles-based fashion designer Bryan Hearns.

It’s fair to say the reality star and her toddler continue to outshine their entire Christmas tree year after year.

3. Halloween cats and dogs

Last October, the pair dressed up in silver cat suits for Halloween as requested by Thompson. Kardashian joked on Instagram: “I know … I know … A cat?! For Halloween. Groundbreaking.”

Original? Perhaps not. But stylish and adorable? Absolutely.

It’s not the first time they dressed up as pets either. For Halloween 2019, the pair did 101 Dalmatians as Cruella de Vil and a Dalmatian puppy.

4. Getting cosy in Skims

The mother-daughter duo recently looked comfy and cute in a matching Skims pyjama set, which they wore during a family photo shoot for the brand.

In fact, they’ve done mini photo shoots for Skims before in support of big sister Kim, as you’d expect. Back in 2019, Thompson was already working the camera alongside her mother for Skims in this snug white ensemble.

5. Editorial chic

It’s no secret that Kardashian loves to twin her fashion with her daughter, so it’s no surprise she pulled Thompson into a photo shoot for her interview with Health Magazine. The pair kept it simple in jeans and white tees.

“The best part of shooting for Health Magazine was my baby girl was able to join me,” she gushed on Instagram afterwards.

6. Bikini babes

Kardashian will find any opportunity to match looks with Thompson, even on holiday. The duo looked summery and chic in matching black and white bikinis during an unknown island holiday back in September 2020. Kardashian wore Onia’s Anna top and Astrid bottoms while little Thompson was kitted out in PQ Swim.

In 2020, they also kept it stylin’ in matching checked Burberry swimwear.

7. Shimmer and shine

Kardashian opted for this show-stopping gold Valdrin Sahiti number for the launch of Hulu’s The Kardashians. Before hitting the red carpet, Kardashian did her very own little photo shoot at home with Thompson in matching gold.

8. Dior darlings

Khloé can’t seem to resist matching designer patterns. In 2021, the pair opted for head-to-toe matching navy and white Dior outfits for the second birthday party of Kim and Kanye West’s son, Psalm.

“Dior darling (Mariah Carey voice),” Khloé captioned the series of mother-daughter photos on social media.

When a fan pointed out how often Kardashian matches outfits with Thompson by compiling an online montage of the pair, the reality star tweeted in reply, “Oh my god, this is the cutest thread, but I did not realise how much I match her. Is she going to hate me when she’s older?”

With a wardrobe that expensive, we highly doubt it!
