Irina Shayk embraces the matchy-matchy tailoring trend in pink suit

July 25, 2024
blog

Irina Shayk is known for her great take on off-duty style – and the model has been photographed around New York City lately looking better than ever, wearing everything from classic trench coats to more fashion-forward new-season pieces. Her latest look? The perfect spring suit.

Earlier this week, Shayk was pictured going about her day, running errands including picking up her four-year-old daughter Lea from school, wearing a pastel pink tailored trouser suit, which she teamed with a matching pink shirt and pink bag.

She accessorised with a pair of black lace-up Doc Martens, which gave a masculine edge to the otherwise girly ensemble. The suit and shirt in question are both from Boss, while the bag is from By Far, and every piece is available to shop right now, below.

Shayk's fun take on spring tailoring is not only a great alternative to the classic summer dress, but it is part of a bigger street-style trend, that which is embracing bright matchy-matchy tailoring.

Shayk herself has donned a few matching trouser and skirt suits of late, while fellow models Kendall Jenner and Hailey Bieber have also been embracing the fun trend with top-to-toe colourful looks that are perfect for this spring weather.
