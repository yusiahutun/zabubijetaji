Lisa Nicole Cloud is a reality star, businesswoman, author, and clothing designer from the United States. Lisa Nicole Cloud is renowned for her appearance in the TV series, “Married to Medicine.”

Early Life

Lisa Nicole Cloud was born on the 15th of October 1973, in Baltimore, Maryland, the United States. Currently, she is 49 years old and has a birth sign of Libra. In addition, she holds US nationality and belongs to British-French-Germanic ethnicity.

She was raised by a single mother after her parents divorced while she was a child. In sixth grade, she relocated to Georgia and had little touch with her father, who only contacted her a few times a year.

Lisa Nicole Cloud – Education

Cloud enrolled at Emory University in 1990. In 1995, she graduated from the University with a bachelor’s degree with honors in arts and a psychology major.

She later attended Johns Hopkins University in 2002. She earned her bachelor’s degree in business administration with honors in 2004.

Professional Career

Lisa Nicole Cloud is regarded as one of a talented entrepreneur, author, and TV personality. She began her professional career as a pharmaceutical sales representative in the healthcare industry. Moreover, she excelled in her role and received praise and recognition for her efforts. She quickly recognized, however, that she desired more than a corporate career. She wanted to leave a legacy and inspire others to do the same.

That’s when she became interested in direct sales and network marketing. She joined a company that provided products and services for personal growth and money creation. Moreover, she used her abilities and knowledge to develop a huge and successful distributor company. She rose to become one of the company’s top earners, earning the title “Ms. Millionaire Maker” for her efforts in assisting others in achieving financial independence.

Likewise, she expanded her entrepreneurial ventures by launching her businesses, including Elite Marketing Strategies, a sales and marketing consulting firm; Boss Network Enterprises, a training and coaching platform for entrepreneurs; and Lisa Nicole Collection, a fashion and beauty line for women of all sizes.

Television Career

Having a successful business career, she is also a well-known television personality. She rose to fame in the reality TV series, “Married to Medicine.” She first appeared on the show in 2014, during the second season, and continued to appear until the fourth season.

Moreover, she was unable to form close ties with the other cast members throughout her stay on set. This had an impact not only on her social life but also on her personal life with her husband. She exited the show in 2016 at the end of the fourth season.

She returned to television in 2020 as the executive producer of “Battle Ready” (2020), a TV movie that tells the experiences of veterans who use martial arts to cope with trauma and adversity. Lisa Nicole Cloud’s passion for creating inspiring and encouraging content that has a good impact on viewers is reflected in her endeavor.
