Relationship Update On Karen Malina White

Karen Malina White, best known for her role as Charmaine Brown in the American television sitcom, "The Cosby Show," has been able to create a fan base of her own, and those admirers are very much interested in knowing about her life.

Well, Karen at the age of 52, who enjoys the net worth of $1 Million, has stayed mysterious for the most parts of her life, so very few details about her personal life have come forth.

Although Karen has not yet married anyone, she was in a relationship once. Karen was previously involved romantically with Malcolm-Jamal Warner back in 2000. 

She shared over seven years of her life with Malcolm; during that course, the couple was even rumored to have gotten married. But that was just a made up story, as back then, Karen gave a statement contradicting the rumors of Malcolm being her husband. She stated,

"I'm not married. People probably started saying that because we were in a relationship for seven and a half years."

Well, even after sharing such extended togetherness, unfortunately, the couple did not make it to the wedding. Karen and Malcolm parted their ways in 2007 after clearing things up with each other.
