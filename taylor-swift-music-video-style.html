Taylor Swift Releases &quot;Style&quot; Music Video -- See Hidden Harry Styles Reference!

The wait is over!

Taylor Swift's new music video for her third single "Style" is here and it's really not what we'd expect from the former country darling. In the vid the "Blank Space" singer takes a more artsy turn, as she sports her trademark red lips and walks barefoot through a forest.

The "Style" video also features flashing images across pieces of shattered glass, and plenty of shirtless moments of Taylor's hot male co-star, Dominic Sherwood.

The 25-year-old premiered the full video on Good Morning America on Friday after teasing scenes from the shoot on her Instagram for the past three days.

The songstress revealed back in September in an interview with Rolling Stone that the song might be about famous ex, Harry Styles. "We should have just called it 'I'm Not Even Sorry,'" Swift remarked slyly referring to the now infamous song.

The video also includes a reference to their relationship, as we get a shot of the paper airplane necklace the two wore when they were dating.
