Summer Miami Luellen: Meet Yung Miami's Daughter

June 21, 2024
blog

Summer Miami Luellen is famous as the second child and only daughter of favorite American rapper Yung Miami. Born Caresha Romeka Brownlee, Yung Miami is part of the renowned rap duo City Girls from Miami, Florida. The rap duo garnered attention after making an uncredited guest appearance on Drake’s chart-topping single “In My Feelings” in 2018. 

Afterward, they released their debut studio album, “Girl Code,” in November 2018. The album featured vocals from stars like Cardi B, Lil Baby, and Jacquees, propelling them to fame. It debuted at number 63 on the Billboard 200 and had two singles, “Twerk” and “Act Up,” peaking at 29 and 26 on the Billboard Hot 100, respectively.

Since she rose to prominence, Yung Miami has been in the media for her successful career and her controversial relationships. However, the curiosity of fans has spilled over to her young daughter. Fans are curious about who Summer Miami Luellen is, who her father is, and if she will be a rapper like her mother.

Here’s everything we know about her.

How Old Is Yung Miami’s Daughter?

Yung Miami’s daughter, Summer Miami Luellen, was born in the United States on October 16, 2019. Hence, she is three years old. When the “Act Up” singer first announced her pregnancy in 2019, she got mixed reactions from fans. While some left congratulatory messages on the now-deleted Instagram post, others were surprised. 

Several months later, the rapper welcomed the arrival of her daughter with a post on her social media accounts. Like most celebrity kids her age, details about Summer are kept private. There are no details regarding her education and educational achievements. However, online reports suggest she is in elementary school. 

Who Is Summer Miami Luellen’s Father?

Yung Miami’s baby daddy is Joshua Howard Luellen, an American rapper, songwriter, and record producer famously known as Southside. Southside debuted as a producer on rapper Waka Floka’s debut album “Flockaveli.” 

Afterward, he co-produced the track “Illest Motherfucker Alive” from Kanye West and Jay Z’s album “Watch the Throne” in 2011 and came into the mainstream hip-hop scene. In addition, he has worked with prominent artists like Rick Ross, B.o.B, Wale, Meek Mill, Lil Scrappy, and MGK, amongst others. 

Southside and Yung Miami started dating in 2018. Even though they had an on-again-off-again relationship for two years, they welcomed their daughter, Summer Miami Luellen, a year into the relationship.

Although fans thought having a child together in 2019 would solidify their relationship, the couple called it quits in 2020. Southside and Yung Miami may no longer be an item, but the former couple remain amicable co-parents for their daughter.

How Many Siblings Does Summer Miami Luellen Have?

Yung Miami’s daughter, Summer Miami Luellen, has six siblings. While she is the second child of the “Strub Tha Ground” singer, she is the youngest of Southside’s six children. Her father has five other children, Karmello Luellen, Legend Luellen, RJ Luellen, Karlye Luellen, and Haylee Luellen, from his previous relationships with other women. 

Similarly, Summer has a brother, Jai Malik Wiggins Jr., from her mother’s relationship with her then-boyfriend, Jai Malik Wiggins Sr., who died in June 2020 from a shootout. Jai Jr. was born on June 26, 2013, and is Yung Miami’s first child and son. Even though they are half-siblings, Summer shares a close bond with her siblings.

Is Yung Miami’s Daughter on Social Media?

Yes, she is. Summer Miami Luellen is a budding social media sensation like many celebrity kids. The adorable girl has a verified Instagram account with over 270 thousand followers and over 100 posts. Summer often shares cute photos of herself with her father, mother, and older brother, Jai Jr. 

Similarly, her mom has a massive social media presence across all platforms. Yung Miami has over 6 million followers on her Instagram account, 1.6 million followers on Twitter, and 1.1 million on TikTok.

What Does Summer Miami Luellen Do for a Living?

Yung Miami’s daughter is a cute little girl in her formative years. She hasn’t stepped into the professional world yet, as she still has much to learn. Fans are eager to see if the celebrity kid will grow into a Hollywood diva that will grace the big screens or a successful rapper dominating music charts like her mom. However, nothing is set in stone for her yet. 

While there’s a good chance she’s earning from her social media popularity, Summer Miami Luellen does not have a net worth yet. However, her famous mom has a net worth of over $5,000,000, which she earned from her career as a successful rap artist, and her podcast, “Caresha Please.”
