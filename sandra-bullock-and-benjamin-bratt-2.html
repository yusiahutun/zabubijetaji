About

Sandra Bullock is a 59 year old American Actress. Born Sandra Annette Bullock on 26th July, 1964 in Arlington County, Virginia, USA, she is famous for her roles as Annie Porter in the 1994 action film Speed and its 1997 action thriller sequel film Speed 2, Angela Bennett in the 1995 action thriller film The Net, Lucy in the 1995 romantic comedy film While You Were Sleeping, Margaret Tate in the 2009 romantic comedy film The Proposal, and Leigh Anne Tuohy in the 2009 biographical sports drama film The Blind Side. Her zodiac sign is Leo.

Benjamin Bratt is a 60 year old American Actor. Born Benjamin George Bratt on 16th December, 1963 in San Francisco, California, USA, he is famous for NYPD Detective Rey Curtis on Law & Order, Dr. Jake Reilly on Private Practice, Steve Navarro on 24: Live Another Day, Jahil Rivera on Star. His zodiac sign is Sagittarius.
