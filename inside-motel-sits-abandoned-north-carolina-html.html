Inside North Carolina's abandoned Family Inn Motel

February 25, 2024
blog

Eerie images and video footage have revealed the abandoned remains of a Tarantino-style Motel that once refused a refund for a room despite the lodgers being robbed at gunpoint.

In scenes that wouldn't be out of place in Pulp Fiction, seedy shots show inside the crumbling buildings where old beds, chairs and other furniture are joined by huge piles of rubble.

Other pictures and footage show the swamp-like pool and a barely readable sign for pool rules.

The snaps and video were taken by American urban explorer The Unknown Cameraman at the Family Inn Motel in North Carolina.

'This was an old roadside motel in North Carolina just down the street from the popular 'South of the Border' attraction,' he said. 'I believe it closed down due to leaky roofs and mold issues,' said the Unknown Cameraman who took the photos and video.

An armed robbery took place at the Family Inn Motel on July 19th 1994 as family of four were travelling from Pennsylvania while traveling to Florida on vacation.

At around 2 a.m., while they were asleep, two men entered through the door of their motel room which did not have a chain or deadbolt. 

One of the men brandished a handgun announcing, 'This is a wake-up call!' and threatened to shoot the family if they could not find any money. The men ripped the phone wires out of the wall.

It could be the lid to the sewer system, or perhaps where a UFO made its final resting spot. Either way, it's rusting away

The outdoors slowly creeps inside where ready-made beds and even a telephone still sit waiting for a call

Mattresses and sofas are piled high. The entire structure looks to be falling apart as time wreaks havoc on the old building 

The restaurant looks to be as enticing as the motel, with plenty of space for parking and outdoor dining

Surely with a good sweep and a bit of Windex, the place could be restored to its former glory? 

It was later revealed that police were in the area but failed to spot the robbery taking place.

One of the thieves walked out to search the family's car, parked near the room at the very time a local police officer drove through the parking lot, waving to the intruder as he drove by.

The robbers left with an ATM card and pin number, $75 in cash, two gold rings and two gold watches.

The family was forced to the floor but luckily suffered no physical injuries. 

After the intruders left, the family went to the front office & the desk clerk called the police.

Despite their ordeal, the motel refused to refund their money for the room but offered them another room.

You just have to dive in! At this stage, the pool filter will be well and truly blocked. Better to fill it in with dirt and start again

The second floor appears to be meeting the first as rotten floorboards finally give way landing on the soft mattresses below

The evidence is all here that time has destroyed what for many was a comfortable, convenient space to stay, save for the occasional armed robbery

No maids or room service here! Corridors are cluttered with fallen ceiling tiled as the place slowly collapses around itself
