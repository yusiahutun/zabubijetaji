'I paid 85 for Grinch visit and my house was trashed and son left drenched in juice'

September 20, 2024
blog

Christmas can be stressful for families at the best of times – but this year’s festive period marks a particularly tense reminder of a traumatic moment for one mum more than most, after she infamously saw her home wrecked by a ‘Grinch’.

Belfast mum Laura Magill had reportedly paid £85 to have the fictional Dr Suess character - famed for his mean-tempered attitude, destructive nature and hatred for Christmas – come to her house to perform for her kids last year.

The entertainer was hired to mess up beds, throw a bit of toilet paper on the tree and pose for pictures.
