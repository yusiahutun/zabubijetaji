Cody Johnson Tacks on 22 More Headlining Dates to Busy 2022 Sched

May 30, 2024
blog

Cody Johnson is striking while the fire is hot. After his first No. 1 hit "'Til You Can't" was certified Platinum, the country singer has added 22 additional Cody Johnson & Friends Tour dates to his 2022 calendar.

Johnson opened the year with a slate of shows alongside Easton Corbin and Ian Munsick. After selling out a handful of those performances, the trio will wrap up their stint on June 18 in Simpsonville, S.C.

As for the rest of the summer and fall, Johnson's calendar is filled with opening gigs and his own headlining shows.

The Texas native's 22 new dates scheduled for August through December will feature Randy Houser. A few will also include Drew Parker as a supporting act.

In addition to his own shows, the "Dear Rodeo" artist is serving as direct support for Luke Combs on his first-ever headlining stadium shows, and will also be opening for Zac Brown Band at their Fenway Park show in Boston and Citi Field in Queens, N.Y.

Johnson also has a slew of fair and festival shows dotted on the calendar.
