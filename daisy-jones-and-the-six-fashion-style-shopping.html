Daisy Jones &amp; the Six best outfits on set: How to dress like the main characters on the show

February 26, 2024
blog

Ever since American musical drama Daisy Jones & The Six premiered on Amazon Prime Video on March 3 earlier this month, we have been able to think of little else.

The miniseries developed by Scott Neustadter and Michael H. Weber, is based on the cult-favourite 2019 novel of the same name by Taylor Jenkins Reid, and takes on the rise and fall of a fictional 1970s rock band. Cue love triangles, stellar vocals from Billy Dunne (aka Sam Claflin) and a performance from Riley Keough so electrifying you’d raise eyebrows for daring to call her a 'Nepo Baby' anymore.

Alongside the dazzling performance of Elvis Presley’s aforementioned granddaughter, there’s also star turns from the likes of Camila Morrone, Suki Waterhouse and a cast which perfectly captures the spirit of the Fleetwood Mac era of music and free love. 

While we wait for the next few episodes to drop we are keeping ourselves entertained by getting our hands on everything and anything 1970s inspired to whet our sartorial appetites.
