Vanessa Bryant’s vacation photos with Pau Gasol and his family went viral this week.

After Kobe Bryant’s ultimately passing, the Gasol family stepped up in a big way to support the Bryant family.

Because of the close bond Kobe and Pau shared, fans absolutely loved seeing the two families unite in the face of tragedy in the way that they did. It spoke to the character of all parties involved.

Pau literally went out of his way to send Vanessa anniversary gifts — that is how deep the relationship ran and continues to run.

This week Vanessa, her daughter Natalia, and the Gasols shared some memorable photos from their vacation together to social media.

“This is amazing! What beautiful pics,” one fan wrote.

“Seriously stunning,” a second person chimed in.

“Love everything about these amazing photos,” a third fan added. “Thanks for sharing!”

This obviously isn’t the first time in recent weeks that Vanessa has found herself in the headlines. Between her wild recent trip photos, memorable weekend pictures and popular outfit choices, she is something of a permanent fixture in the news cycle.

Although she was always a big name, the way that she has handled her duties as the matriarch of the Bryant family has turned Vanessa into a major role model worldwide.
