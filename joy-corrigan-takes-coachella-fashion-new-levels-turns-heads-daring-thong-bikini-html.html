Joy Corrigan takes Coachella fashion to new levels as she turns heads in a VERY daring thong bikini

March 18, 2024
blog

She's known for storming the runways of Victoria's Secret.

And Joy Corrigan turned heads once again as she strolled through the desert in a very daring ensemble while attending Coachella Festival in Indio, California, over the weekend.

The model, 28, displayed her extremely toned stomach in a blue and pink bikini top and yellow bottoms.

The racy swimwear featured a low-cut bandeau top and a high waisted thong.

Joy bared all in the daring number, which she teamed which denim-style thigh-high boots for her day in the desert.

The model toted her belongings in a tie-dye blue bag and accessorised with body chains and gold necklaces.

She added a pair of purple sunglasses to her look as she strutted her stuff, while accessorising with a butterfly clip pinned to her hair.

Joy has been enjoying the weekend with a collection of daring outfits and went rollerblading during a break from attending the festival. 

The Victoria's Secret alum channelled her inner Barbie in a daring pink thong bodysuit.

Joy showcased her lengthy pins in the high-cut one-piece, while fitting in some exercise during her time.

The model is among the roughly 250,000 attendees who flocked to the highly-popular festival this year to watch Bad Bunny, Blackpink and Frank Ocean perform.

Other artists who performed at the festival were Calvin Harris, Icelandic star Bjork, Spain's Rosalia, Nigeria's Burna Boy and British-Indian singer Jai Paul.  

Kaytranada, Porter Robinson, Yaeji and vets Chemical Brothers, Underworld and Sasha & John Digweed were also there. 

Cheeky! The model, 28, displayed her extremely toned stomach and peachy behind in a blue and pink bikini top and yellow thong bottoms

Alt acts were Gorillaz, Wet Leg, Boygenius (featuring Phoebe Bridgers, Lucy Dacus and Julien Baker), Charli XCX, Remi Wolf, Ethel Cain, Christine and the Queens, Weyes Blood, Muna, Snail Mail, Sudan Archives and veterans Blondie and the Breeders. 

Bad Bunny is one of the world's most popular artists and in 2022, he earned as much as $435.38 million from his 'World's Hottest Tour'.

The rap star, 29, actually surpassed the previous record, which was set by Ed Sheeran for his Divide tour in 2018, when he earned $432.3 million.

Bad Bunny - whose real name is Benito Ocasio - also became the most streamed artist on Spotify for the third year in a row.

In 2022 alone, his music was actually streamed more than 18.5 billion times.

Coachella takes place at the Empire Polo Grounds in Indio, California on the weekends of April 14-16 and April 21-23.

Last year's headliners were Harry Styles, Billie Eilish, and the Weeknd as well as Swedish House Mafia. Doja Cat, Lil Baby, Phoebe Bridgers, Megan Thee Stallion, 21 Savage were there too.
