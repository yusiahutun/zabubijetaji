C3 Presents Announces Noah Kahan And Foo Fighters For Connecticuts Soundside Music Festival

February 01, 2024
blog

BRIDGEPORT, CT (CelebrityAccess) – The lineup for the inaugural Soundside Music Festival has been revealed, with Grammy-nominated/New England native Noah Kahan taking the stage on Saturday and the legendary Foo Fighters performing on Sunday. The two-day festival will take place along the shoreline of the Long Island Sound at Seaside Park on September 28 – 29. Grace Potter, Boyz II Men, The Kills, Norah Jones, Goo Goo Dolls, Fleet Foxes, and many more are also scheduled to perform.

Connecticut’s biggest music festival, Sound On Sound, is now under a new name alongside promoters C3 Presents (Austin City Limits Music Festival, Lollapalooza, Sea.Hear.Now, Gov Ball, and more). Soundside continues the incredible Sound On Sound 2023 experience and feel ~ with its unique rotating single-stage setup alongside gourmet food options from some of the surrounding area’s best establishments, including PopUp Bagels, The Gotham Burger Social Club, Grumpy Dumpling Co., 29 Markle Ct, The Blind Rhino, Walter’s Hot Dogs, a curated selection of cocktails, craft beer and wine options and much more. The complete food and beverage lineup will be announced at a later date.

“I am very excited that Bridgeport is once again the host to this year’s Soundside Music Festival, formally known as Sound On Sound,” says Mayor Joe Ganim of Bridgeport, CT. “In past years, this festival has drawn in over 50,000 visitors. I am sure this year; it will again draw tens of thousands who will get to experience all that Bridgeport has to offer. This is another example of how Bridgeport is ‘on the move’ as we strengthen our legacy of being Connecticut’s music and entertainment capital.”

Fans can sign up now for the SMS Presale tomorrow (May 16) from 10 am – 12 pm ET. This is the ONLY WAY to guarantee getting your tickets at the lowest price. Sign up for a presale passcode to secure your spot at the Soundside Music Festival. All ticket prices will increase tomorrow at noon EST when the public on-sale begins. Ticket prices are all-inclusive with no fees on top. Ticket offerings include General Admission, GA+, VIP and Skydeck. All ticket types offer 1-day and 2-day options. Parking will also be available for purchase.

Layaway Plans are also available on all ticket types, starting at $25 down. Also, local Bridgeport residents (06604, 06605, 06606, 06607, 06608, and 06610 zip codes) are eligible for a 15% discount on tickets on a first-come, first-served basis.
