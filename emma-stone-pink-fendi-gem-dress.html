Emma Stone's Little Pink Dress Is Made Up of Glittering Gems

September 24, 2024
blog

Emma Stone made the most glittering entrance at the 14th Annual Governors Awards last night, in a couture gown entirely covered in gems.

The sweet little pink party dress was courtesy of Fendi’s Fall/Winter 2023 couture collection, and featured a slouchy, off-the-shoulder neckline, oversize sleeves, a sharp torso cutout, and an over-the-knee skirt. Adorning the piece were hundreds of circular gems in subtle tones: clear, Cinderella blue, pistachio green, baby pink.

Stone wore the incredible piece with shiny nude heels with a metallic gold point, various dainty gold rings, and diamond and emerald stud earrings. Her glowy, rosy makeup went perfectly with the pink minidress, and she had her hair styled in a pretty bun.

Michael Buckner//Getty Images

The Oscar-winning actor has been on quite a red-carpet style streak as of late.

On Sunday, she wore a different bejeweled gown to the 2024 Golden Globes, where she took home a Best Female Actor award for her performance in Poor Things. The sophisticated nude dress was a custom Louis Vuitton design composed of a sheer slip with so many glittery champagne-colored sequins embroidered all over, along with a slew of dainty pale pink and dusty blue flower illustrations. Stone wore it with only a pair of satin nude heeled sandals, diamond double drop earrings, and a vibrant yellow gemstone ring.

And on Monday, the La La Land star attended the season-finale premiere of her comedy-drama series The Curse in a sheer black dress pulled straight from Givenchy’s Spring/Summer 2024 ready-to-wear runway. The breezy, gauzy piece was embellished with a rosette and featured the daintiest spaghetti straps.
