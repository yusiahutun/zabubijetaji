Canada Goose has long reigned supreme when it comes to winter coats and jackets. The brand has easily become a symbol of both status and style, and has even been named the unofficial jacket of film sets (according to Business Insider, plenty of Game of Thrones folks wore them off-camera). Its craftsmanship is some of the best around for those who dwell in colder climates, but it doesn’t come without a hefty price tag.

Some Canada Goose options retail for as high as $1,700, which is quite the fee to pay if you don’t plan on utilizing your jacket all year-round. If you’re not looking to invest that much on your 2024 winter gear but still want a chic (and, of course, incredibly warm) design to bundle up in, one retailer has a surprising amount of Canada Goose lookalikes. Best of all, they’ll cost you hundreds less than the name brand.
