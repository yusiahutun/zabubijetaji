There's no keeping a Jersey girl down.

In between starring in her own reality show Snooki & JWoww and planning her wedding, Nicole 'Snooki' Polizzi is pushing her luxurious body products and tanning lotions.

The petite, 4ft 8in star posed with her line of creams and lotions at the Smart Tan Downtown Convention at the Nashville Convention Center in Nashville, Tennessee on Saturday.

Product placement: Nicole 'Snooki' Polizzi posed with her line of beauty products at the Smart Tan Downtown Convention at the Nashville Convention Center on Saturday

It's probably just as well the Dancing With The Stars contestant was voted off the popular show because it's hard to see how Snooki would have fitted everything in.

She hosted her own meet and greet at the event, which runs for three days.

Looking stunning in a tight-fitting, flash tone mini-dress, the 25-year-old was dripping gold from the metal shoulder detail on her outfit to the heavy, double strand gold necklace and matching earrings and bracelet.

Heavy metal: The 25-year-old glittered in a weighty gold necklace and matching earrings and bracelet while her flesh-toned mini-dress featured a gold detail on the shoulders

Showing her range: The reality star exercised her arm with a green weight while promoting her tanning lotions

Her fingers twinkled with a selection of large rings and her nails were painted in various hues of red.

During the day she changed into a revealing black and gold Daisy Dukes and a silky black top to shoot a commercial for her tanning range.

Among the redhead's pricey products are Snooki bronzers for $75, body scrubs and a moisturising body wash.

Meet and greet: Snooki spent time with her fans at the convention

The former Jersey Shore star got engaged to her entrepreneur boyfriend Jionni Lavalle, 26, in March 2012 shortly after she found out she was pregnant. Their son Lorenzo was born five months later on August 29.

Since then Snooki has tossed out a few hints when the big day will happen.

But on Wednesday the celebrity revealed she had started planning her wedding and shared the news with her Twitter followers along with a photo of her holding her little black book.
