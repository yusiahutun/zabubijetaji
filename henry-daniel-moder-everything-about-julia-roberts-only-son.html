Henry Daniel Moder: Everything About Julia Roberts Only Son

Henry Daniel Moder is one of many celebrity kids who became instantly famous from birth. Here’s a look at his life.

Julia Roberts and Danny Moder have a lovely son who has recently piqued fans’ interest. Keep reading to learn all about Henry Daniel Moder.

Who Is Henry Daniel Moder?

Henry Daniel Moder was born on June 18, 2007, to celebrity parents Julia Roberts and Daniel Richard Moder. He is the youngest child of the couple and also their only son. Henry is all grown up now, and fans can not believe how big he has gotten. 

His mother, Julia, was pregnant with Henry while filming the drama “Fireflies in the Garden” and reflected on what it was like to see her baby bump on the big screen years after welcoming her youngest child. “When I see it now, he’s this big boy who can dress himself and do all these things,” she told People in October 2011. “It really is touching to see that.” 

His father, Danny, has some posts of Henry on his various adventures on his page, showing the world his kid’s growth. Henry is said to be a thrill-seeker and an adventurous soul. The videos shared on Danny’s Instagram page show that the youngster participates in extreme yet exciting sports such as BMX and surfing. 

In a post in May 2021, Danny shared a video of Henry on his morning commute to school riding his skateboard, and this video made headlines on blogs, with fans impressed by the young chap’s incredible talent. The video has gathered over 200,000 views with thousands of comments as well. 

The following month, Danny posted another video of his son nailing some skateboarding tricks at a skate park. The celebrity kid was dressed in a T-shirt and a baseball cap and looked much like his dad. At first glance, fans were confused and then in awe of the striking resemblance between Danny and Henry. He has been tagged his father’s “mini-me” ever since. 

Who Are Henry Daniel Moder’s Parents?

Henry is a “royal” celebrity kid as both his parents are famous in their respective fields. The couple married in 2002 and have been together ever since. Although both parents work in the entertainment industry, they are professionals in different areas. 

Julia Roberts 

Henry’s mother is an iconic American actress who made her Hollywood debut in the early 1980s. She was born on October 28, 1967, and developed an early passion for acting. 

Julia Roberts is one of Hollywood’s leading actresses known for her versatility and exceptional talent. She has featured in various movies over the years, from romantic comedies to serious dramas and tearjerkers, and has proven that there is no role she cannot embody.

Her movies have grossed over $3.9 billion at the global box office, making her one of the highest-grossing actresses ever. Some of her blockbusters include:

  • Pretty Woman
  • Ocean’s Eleven
  • Notting Hill
  • Ocean’s Twelve
  • Runaway Bride
  • Wonder
  • Hook
  • My Best Friend’s Wedding

Her successful career has been decorated with wins and nominations from the industry’s most significant awards shows, including winning an Academy Award, a British Academy Film Award, and three Golden Globe Awards. 

Danny Moder

Daniel Richard Moder, born January 31, 1969, is an American cinematographer. Like his wife, Danny has been highly successful in the movie industry. He is the ‘eyes’ behind some Hollywood blockbusters, including “Secret in Their Eyes,” “The Mexican,” and “Fireflies in the Garden.”

He has also bagged major industry awards like a Primetime Emmy Award nomination for his cinematography in the television film “The Normal Heart.” Danny has been a cinematographer for about 30 years, producing over 40 movies and TV shows. 

Is Henry Daniel Moder an Only Child?

The young lad is not the only child of his parents. He is the youngest of three children, with a set of twin sisters before him. The twin sisters, Hazel and Phinnaeus Moder, were born in November 2004.

All three kids are now in their teenage years, but much about their personal lives remains unknown. Their parents prefer to keep their children’s lives away from the public because they want them to grow up in a “normal” environment. 

Henry Daniel Moder’s Net Worth

Henry is still in his teenage years and is not known to be financially independent yet. Therefore, he has no net worth. His parents, however, have a large fortune to their name. His mum is estimated to be worth $250 million, while his dad is estimated to be worth $12 million.  

The world is Henry Daniel Moder’s oyster, and we cannot wait to see what the future holds for him.
